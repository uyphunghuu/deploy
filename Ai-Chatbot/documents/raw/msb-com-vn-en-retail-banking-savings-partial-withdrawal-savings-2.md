## Product information
Overview  Eligibility criteria  Application Guide   
| **General Overview**  | A savings product that allows a single deposit with multiple partial principal withdrawals during the savings period. The remaining balance continues to earn interest at the original deposit tenor rate.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Tenor**  | 1-13, 15, 18, 24, and 36 months  |  
| **Currency**  | VND   |  
| **Minimum Deposit Amount**  | VND 20,000,000  |  
| **Minimum Withdrawal Amount**  | VND 5,000,000 for over-the-counter deposits and VND 500,000 for online deposits  |  
| **Maximum Number of Withdrawals**  | Up to 5 withdrawals for over-the-counter deposits; Unlimited withdrawals for online partial principal withdrawal deposits  |  
| **Interest Payment Method**  | At maturity  |  
| **Early Withdrawal**  | Customers may make early withdrawals (partial or full). The early-withdrawn portion earns non-term deposit interest rates. The remaining principal continues to earn interest at the original deposit tenor rate.  |  
| **Renewal**  | Automatic rollover of both principal and interest into a new corresponding tenor, or conversion to the Highest Interest Rate Savings product with an equivalent tenor if the Bank discontinues the Partial Principal Withdrawal product. Interest rates applied will be those published at the time of renewal by MSB.  |  
| **Eligible Applicants**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Application Requirements**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| **How to Open a Partial Withdrawal Savings Account at Branches**  | 1. Prepare required documents: Bring your original, valid identification documents: chip-based Citizen ID / Level-2 electronic Citizen ID / Passport. 2. Visit an MSB branch: Go to the nearest branch or transaction office of MSB. 3. Request and complete the application: Inform the bank staff of your request to open a Partial Withdrawal Savings account. The staff will provide and guide you through the required application form. 4. Deposit funds and receive confirmation: Make the deposit and receive a physical savings passbook or confirmation via the banking app.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **How to Open a Partial Withdrawal Savings Account Online**  |  1. Log in to the MSB Digital Bank app on your mobile device. 2. Select “Deposits & Investments.” 3. Choose “Open a New Term Deposit” and select the deposit product that suits your needs. 4. Enter the required information:
  * Source account
  * Deposit amount (subject to the minimum deposit requirement)
  * Deposit tenor (e.g. 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, etc.) and applicable interest rate

Maturity instructions and other options (if any) 5. Tick “Agree” to the Terms & Conditions to proceed. 6. Review the information and authenticate the transaction to open the deposit. 7. The system will notify you of a successful transaction. You can monitor your savings account directly in the app.  |

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Can customers withdraw funds before maturity multiple times during the savings period?
18/03/2026
Yes. With the **Partial Withdrawal Savings** product, customers can flexibly withdraw funds before maturity multiple times:
  * **Up to 5 withdrawals** for savings accounts opened **at branches**
  * **Unlimited withdrawals** for **online** savings accounts


What is the enhanced security layer for savings accounts?
12/02/2026
Customers will have an additional protection layer when closing online savings accounts through security questions. Customers may select up to 3 security questions. When enabling or disabling the feature, customers must correctly answer the security questions set at the time of service registration.
Can customers authorize another person to close their savings account?
03/02/2026
Yes. MSB allows customers to **authorize another person to close a savings account opened at a branch** , in accordance with the bank’s current regulations.

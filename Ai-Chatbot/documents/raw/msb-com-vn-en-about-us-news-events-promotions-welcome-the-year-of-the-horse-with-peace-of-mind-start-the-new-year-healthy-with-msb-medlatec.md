# Welcome the Year of the Horse with peace of mind – Start the new year healthy with MSB & Medlatec
03/02/2026
Validity Period 30/06/2026  (Ongoing) 
Understanding that robust health is the foundation of a peaceful year, MSB collaborates with MEDLATEC to offer convenient financial solutions combined with practical healthcare gifts to ensure a perfect start to the Year of the Horse.
![](https://axkujhcqrnim.compat.objectstorage.ap-singapore-1.oraclecloud.com/msbweb-bucket/msb-website/2026/03/54bedbec-a74a-3519-c666-03a3f9102482.png)  
Understanding that robust health is the foundation of a peaceful year, MSB collaborates with MEDLATEC to offer convenient financial solutions combined with practical healthcare gifts to ensure a perfect start to the Year of the Horse.
### **1. Program 1: Receive an e-voucher for a 3-parameter blood test when opening a payment account at MSB**
Open a payment account at MSB today to receive:
50,000 VND credited to your payment account
E-voucher for a 3-parameter blood test for diabetes risk screening & Cardiovascular check-up worth VND 145,000 at Medlatec – This is a suitable option for quickly checking important health indicators, helping you start the new year with peace of mind and a strong physique.
How to get the offer:
– Step 1: Download the MSB app: https://mbank.onelink.me/lK5E/u46b2cmh
– Step 2: Open an account > Enter the code “MED” in the Referral Code section.
– Step 3: Make one transfer transaction of 50,000 VND or more.
Applicable until June 30, 2026.
Program terms and conditions: See details in  href=”https://www.msb.com.vn/documents/20121/8097933/Th%E1%BB%83+l%E1%BB%87+CTKM+t%E1%BA%B7ng+evoucher+x%C3%A9t+nghi% E1%BB%87m+3+ch%E1%BB%89+s%E1%BB%91+t%E1%BA%A1i+Medlatec.pdf/c64106ca-99bc-ea2b-6b1f-3c41cd531556?t=1767699247064″>at here.
### **2. Program 2: Receive an e-voucher for a blood test (18 indicators and 2 liver enzyme indicators) when opening a payment account at MSB**
Open an MSB payment account and receive an attractive financial and health gift:
Receive 50,000 VND into your payment account
E-voucher for a blood test (18 indicators and 2 liver enzyme indicators) at Medlatec worth 155,000 VND – A health gift from Medlatec to help you get a more comprehensive assessment of your body, detect liver diseases early, and check your blood status.
How to receive the offer:
– Step 1: Download the MSB app: https://mbank.onelink.me/lK5E/u46b2cmh
**– Step 2:** Open an account > Enter code “MED2” in the Referral Code section
**– Step 3:** Make one transfer transaction of 50,000 VND or more
_Applicable period: until June 30, 2026._
**Program rules:** See details [here](https://www.msb.com.vn/documents/20121/8097933/Th%E1%BB%83+l%E1%BB%87+CTKM+t%E1%BA%B7ng+evoucher+x%C3%A9t+nghi%E1%BB%87m+18+ch%E1%BB%89+s%E1%BB%91+t%E1%BA%A1i+Medlatec.pdf/0e92e7da-7b9b-0ebb-c343-94cfd426470c?t=1767699344957).

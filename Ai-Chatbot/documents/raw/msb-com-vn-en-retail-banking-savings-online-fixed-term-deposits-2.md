## Product information
Product Information  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide   
| **General Overview**  | A savings product that allows customers to deposit and manage funds entirely online via internet banking.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Tenor**  | A wide range of tenors, depending on the savings product selected by the customer.  |  
| **Currency**  | VND  |  
| **Minimum Deposit Amount**  | From VND 1,000,000  |  
| **Interest Payment Method**  | Various interest payment options are available—such as one-time interest payment, monthly periodic interest payment, or interest payment at maturity—depending on the savings product selected.  |  
| **Maturity**  | For the auto-renewal option: on the maturity date, if the customer does not close the term deposit, the system will automatically roll over the deposit into a new term with the interest rate published at MSB branches/website at the time of renewal by MSB.  |  
| **Eligible Applicants**  | Vietnamese citizens who are residents, aged 18 and above.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Application Requirements**  | Customers must hold a payment account with MSB.  |  
**Step 1** : Log in to the MSB Digital Bank app on your mobile device.
**Step 2** : Find and select “Deposits & Investments.”
**Step 3** : Select “Open a New Term Deposit” and choose the deposit product that suits your needs.
**Step 4** : Enter the required information:
  * Source account
  * Deposit amount (subject to the minimum deposit requirement)
  * Deposit tenor (e.g. 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, etc.) and applicable interest rate
  * Maturity instructions and other options (if any)


**Step 5** : Proceed by ticking “Agree” to the Terms & Conditions.
**Step 6** : Review the details and authenticate the transaction to open the deposit.
**Step 7** : The system will notify you of a successful transaction. You can then monitor your savings account directly in the app.

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Can I choose which savings accounts to apply the enhanced security layer to?
12/02/2026
Yes. Customers may select one or multiple online savings accounts (up to 10 accounts per request).
What is the enhanced security layer for savings accounts?
12/02/2026
Customers will have an additional protection layer when closing online savings accounts through security questions. Customers may select up to 3 security questions. When enabling or disabling the feature, customers must correctly answer the security questions set at the time of service registration.
Can customers withdraw funds before maturity multiple times during the savings period?
18/03/2026
Yes. With the **Partial Withdrawal Savings** product, customers can flexibly withdraw funds before maturity multiple times:
  * **Up to 5 withdrawals** for savings accounts opened **at branches**
  * **Unlimited withdrawals** for **online** savings accounts

Installment loans, consumer loans, and get instant disbursement with the CUB Vietnam App. CUB Vietnam App – A Comprehensive Financial Solution that is Fast, Convenient, Reliable, and 100% Online. Choose an easy and stress-free loan designed just for you, skipping the rigid multi-step approval process with the CUB Vietnam App. Features include: --- APP FEATURES--- • Apply for a 100% online consumer loan with just Citizen ID (CCCD or CC), with attractive interest rates. • Quick loan approval. • Fast loan disbursement with simple steps. • Online credit limits up to 40 million VND. • Flexible installment terms from 3 to 36 months. • Flat yearly interest rate is 22.92%. ---LOAN REQUIREMENTS--- • Product is only applied for Vietnamese citizen. • Age between 22 and 55 years old. • Have a Citizen ID (CCCD or CC) with embedded chip. • Have a registered phone number under your name. ---ILLUSTRATIVE EXAMPLE--- • Online credit limit amount: 40,000,000 VND • Loan term: 24 months • Flat yearly interest rate: 22.92% (based on flat interest rates and may differ slightly from actual rates). This interest rate is for reference only, kindly check the CUB's Fee and Interest Rate Schedule from time to time for more details.) • Monthly payment: 2,447,509 VND (equivalent to 81,584 VND/day) ---DATA SECURITY FEATURES--- CUB Vietnam is a fully secured mobile application developed by Cathay United Bank– Ho Chi Minh City Branch. The app is approved by Apple App Store with international FIDO certification, offering dual protection against unauthorized access. • Biometric authentication: Enable fingerprint or Face ID unlock for enhanced security. • One-Time Password (OTP): Enter the OTP sent to your registered phone number for an extra security layer. • Regular security checks: Improve account security and minimize risks of unauthorized access. Customer privacy is CUB Vietnam's top priority. We strictly comply with the provisions of the law on personal data protection, ensuring your data is safeguarded. Learn more about CUB Vietnam's privacy policy on our website. ________________________________________ REQUIREMENTS FOR USING THE CUB VIETNAM APP • Camera: Required to capture your ID and facial image. • Turned on Location services: Mandatory for disbursement and signing electronic contracts. • NFC-enabled phone: Used to scan Citizen ID cards with embedded chip. • Biometric login: Enable biometric login for faster app access.

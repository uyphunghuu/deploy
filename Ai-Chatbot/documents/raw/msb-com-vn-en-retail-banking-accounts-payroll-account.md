## Product information
Overview  Eligibility criteria  Application Guide  Forms & fees   
| **General Overview**  | A comprehensive financial solution for employees working at organizations and enterprises that have signed payroll disbursement agreements with MSB.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Product Benefits**  |  – Optimized salary cash flow, free of charge for all transactions, with easy access to credit facilities without income verification – Credit or overdraft limits of up to 5 times monthly income for:
  * Employees working at organizations/enterprises with Mpayroll agreements with MSB, earning an average monthly income of at least VND 10 million in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, or VND 8 million in other provinces/cities
  * Salary fully credited via bank transfer.

– Comprehensive fee waivers, including card issuance fees, annual fees for payment accounts and debit cards, internet banking/mobile banking fees, internal and 24/7 interbank transfer fees via internet banking, on-us and off-us ATM transaction fees, monthly account maintenance fees, and SMS service fees (if notifications are received via app notifications)  |  
| **Account Opening at Branches**  | 
  * Individual customers who are Vietnamese nationals or foreign nationals, in accordance with MSB’s regulations.

 |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Online Account Opening via the App**  | 
  * Applicable to Vietnamese citizens holding a single nationality and legally residing in Vietnam
  * Customers aged 18 and above who do not yet have a payment account with MSB
  * The mobile phone number used for service registration has not been previously used, or is no longer in use, for registering MSB products or services

 |  
  * For Corporate Customers: Please contact MSB staff at the nearest transaction point for assistance.
  * For Individual Customers: Customers can open a payment account on the MSB Digital Bank app and register it as their salary account with their company.
  * Guide to Opening a Payment Account on the MSB Digital Bank App 
    * Step 1: Download the MSB Digital Bank app from the app store
    * Step 2: Register your login credentials
    * Step 3: Select “Open Account”

## Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any fees for opening an account?
11/03/2026
Not at all. Account registration is completely free of charge.
Can I change the default account used for transfers?
12/02/2026
Yes, you can change the default account for transfers.  
To change the default account, please follow these steps:
  * **Step 1** : Log in to the MSB mBank application.
  * **Step 2** : Select Settings → Payment Settings.
  * **Step 3** : Choose Change Default Account and follow the instructions.


Can I still use the Business Household Account if I am not familiar with technology?
03/02/2026
Absolutely. The Business Household Account is designed to be simple and user-friendly with an intuitive interface.
Even users who are not familiar with technology can easily receive payments, track transactions, and manage revenues.

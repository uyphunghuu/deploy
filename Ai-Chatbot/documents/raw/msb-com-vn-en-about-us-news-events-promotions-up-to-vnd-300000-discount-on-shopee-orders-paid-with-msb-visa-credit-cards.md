# Up to VND 300,000 Discount on Shopee Orders Paid with MSB Visa Credit Cards
05/05/2026
period of application 01/03/2026 - 30/09/2026  (Ongoing) 
**Up to VND 300,000 Discount on Shopee Orders Paid with MSB Visa Credit Cards**
**Offer Details:****  
** The promotion applies to orders paid using MSB Visa Credit Cards (BIN starting with number 4), with the following mechanics:
  * Program 01: Discount of VND 100,000 for orders from VND 1,500,000 on double-date campaigns
  * Program 02: Discount of VND 50,000 for orders from VND 800,000 every Friday
  * Program 03: Discount of VND 300,000 for orders from VND 1,800,000 from the 28th to 30th of each month


**Promotion Period:**
  * Program 01: From 09:00 to 23:59 on 02/02/2026, 03/03/2026, 04/04/2026, 05/05/2026, 06/06/2026, 07/07/2026, 08/08/2026, and 09/09/2026
  * Program 02: From 09:00 to 23:59 every Friday, from 01 March 2026 to 30 September 2026
  * Program 03: From 12:00 to 23:59 from the 28th to 30th of each month, from 01 March 2026 to 30 September 2026


_The program will end when the promotion period expires or when the allocated promotional quota is fully utilized, whichever comes first._
**Eligible Customers:****  
** Customers who log in to their accounts on the Shopee website or mobile application and select Visa credit card payment (MSB Visa Credit Cards with BIN starting with number 4 or other eligible international Visa cards).
**Terms and Conditions:**
  * Customers must link their Visa card and select it as the payment method on Shopee.
  * At checkout, select “Credit/Debit Card” and choose a valid linked card to complete the transaction.
  * The discount will be automatically applied to eligible transactions (no promo code required).
  * Applicable only to purchases of physical products on Shopee; not applicable to mobile SIM cards, top-up cards, e-vouchers, services, infant milk products for children aged 0–24 months, seller-arranged shipping orders, or bundled promotional products (e.g., buy 1 get 1, buy 2 get 10% off, etc.).
  * Each card is eligible for the offer once per month per program.
  * Offers are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis within each promotional time frame. Customers may not receive the discount if the allocated budget has been exhausted.
  * The program may end earlier than scheduled if the promotional quota is fully utilized.
  * Can be used in conjunction with other Shopee promotional vouchers, unless otherwise specified.
  * The offer is non-transferable, non-exchangeable for cash, and cannot be combined with other promotions unless explicitly stated.
  * Visa and the service provider reserve the right to suspend, cancel, or amend the offer and its terms and conditions at any time without prior notice.
  * The offer is provided based on information supplied by the merchant.

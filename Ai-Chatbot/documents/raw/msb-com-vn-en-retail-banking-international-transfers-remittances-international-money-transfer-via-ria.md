## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Do I have to pay any fees when receiving remittances via RIA at MSB?
14/03/2026
No. Customers are not required to pay any fees when receiving remittances via RIA at MSB.
Do I have to wait long when making an international transfer via RIA?
13/03/2026
Once customers provide **complete and valid documentation** , the transaction will be processed within **approximately 20–30 minutes** , and the recipient can receive the money shortly after.
How can I transfer money to my child who is currently abroad via RIA?
13/03/2026
Please follow the steps below:
  * Step 1: Choose and visit the nearest MSB branch to perform the transaction.  
Step 2: Present your identification documents and provide valid transfer documentation.  
Step 3: Complete the required information and sign the confirmation on MSB’s money transfer form.  
Step 4: Make the transaction payment.  
Step 5: Receive the transaction receipt and the transfer reference number (PIN).

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
What is the annual fee for the debit card? What is the usage limit?
14/03/2026
When becoming an M-First Priority member, customers will be issued the Mastercard Platinum FCB international debit card with a cash withdrawal limit of up to VND 250 million per day, and the annual card fee will be waived as long as the Priority membership tier is maintained.
Will I earn reward points when spending with this debit card?
14/03/2026
Yes. Customers who are Priority Banking members will earn 40 points for every VND 200,000 spent, with a maximum accumulation of 10,000 M-Points per month.
Which cards can be linked to Apple Pay?
12/02/2026
Currently, all active international credit cards and international debit cards issued by MSB can be linked to Apple Pay, provided that the cards are in normal operating status.

# Amended terms and conditions: Promotion program for “new to bank” individual customers 2025 – 2026 (NTB 2025, 2026)
27/02/2026
Validity Period 09/12/2025 - 30/04/2026  (Expired) 
Location Nationwide
Eligible Customers Individual customer
**1. SCOPE OF AMENDMENT**
This document serves to amend and supplement certain articles of the Promotion Program for Individual Customers who are New to Bank (NTB) for the 2025-2026 period, specifically focusing on customers participating in investment and wealth products for the first time.
**2. AMENDED CONTENT**
**2.1. Extension of Program Duration**
  * **Old duration:** From January 1, 2025, to December 31, 2025.
  * **New duration:** From January 1, 2025, to **December 31, 2026**.


**2.2. Updated Incentive Structure for First-Time Participants**
The reward structure for eligible customers is updated as follows:  
| **No.**  | **Product Category**  | **Minimum Transaction Amount (VND)**  | **Minimum Holding Period**  | **Reward Value (VND)**  |  
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |  
| 1  | Term Savings  | 500,000,000  | 06 months  | 200,000  |  
| 2  | Certificates of Deposit (CD)  | 1,000,000,000  | 12 months  | 500,000  |  
| 3  | Open-ended Fund Certificates  | 100,000,000  | N/A  | 100,000  |  
**3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA**
  * Customers must be identified as **New to Bank (NTB)** within the program period.
  * Customers have not incurred any transactions in the “Wealth Product” group (Savings, CD, Bonds, Fund Certificates) at MSB prior to the program’s effective date.
  * Each customer is eligible for a **one-time reward** for their first transaction during the program period.


**4. GENERAL PROVISIONS**
  * All other terms and conditions specified in the original program announcement remain unchanged unless explicitly modified by this document.
  * MSB reserves the right to terminate the program early if the promotional budget is fully utilized before the end of the specified duration.
  * In case of any disputes, MSB’s decision shall be final and binding, in accordance with the law.


**5. EFFECTIVE DATE**
These amendments and supplements take effect from **January 1, 2026** , and are an integral part of the overall “NTB 2025, 2026” campaign.

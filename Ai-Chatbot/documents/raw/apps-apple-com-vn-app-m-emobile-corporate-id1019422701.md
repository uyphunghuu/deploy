Welcome to Mobile banking services (M-eMobile) for corporate customers. With Mobile Banking, Maritime Bank brings Customer a completely different experience. In the first phrase, MSB focuses on deploy applications to meet the emphasis demand of approval level on approving financial transactions. Only with a phone with Internet access, Customer can approve transactions, including: - Transfer: Single transaction in VND inside and outside the MSB’s system. - Batch Transactions/ Salary: Make payments to partners and customers in batches. Salary officials and employees in the enterprise - Open / close deposit accounts online and make other transactions. Besides providing approving financial transaction features for Customers, M-eMobile also supports additional features: - Search for ATM, nearest MSB branch, determine the current position of Customer on the map. (Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life) - Look up exchange rates and calculate the currency exchange rate quickly and accurately. - Provide information of individual promotions and offers by MSB. - Online support: MSB will contact you after receiving requests from Customer. (These features also apply to users who enter transactions)

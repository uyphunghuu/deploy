# Bestowing gratitude, sending peace of mind with MSB x Prudential
29/05/2026
period of application 01/06/2026 - 30/06/2026  (Ongoing) 
Dear Valued Customers,
Insurance renewal is not only about maintaining protection benefits, but also a way for you to continue preserving peace of mind for yourself and your family. In Q2.2026, MSB would like to express gratitude to customers who accompany Prudential Insurance products through the program **“Accompanying Gratitude Gifts Q2.2026 – Phase 2”**
  1. **Promotion period:** from 01/06/2026 to the end of 30/06/2026
  2. **Applicable products:**


  * All products except for PRU – AN TÂM BẢO TÍN product


  1. **Promotional gifts:**


  * **Got It E-voucher worth 2,500,000 VND** for the periodic Actual Collected Insurance Premium or Actual Collected Insurance Premium to successfully restore Policy validity of 01 valid Policy from 60 million VND and above
  * **Got It E-voucher worth 1,500,000 VND** for the periodic Actual Collected Insurance Premium or Actual Collected Insurance Premium to successfully restore Policy validity of 01 valid Policy from 40 million VND to under 60 million VND
  * **Got It E-voucher worth 500,000 VND** for the periodic Actual Collected Insurance Premium or Actual Collected Insurance Premium to successfully restore Policy validity of 01 valid Policy from 20 million VND to under 40 million VND


For promotion program details, please see here. Please contact hotline: 1900 6083 or visit the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office for free consultation support.

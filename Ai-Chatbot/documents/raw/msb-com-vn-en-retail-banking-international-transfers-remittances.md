## Frequently Asked Questions

Can exchange agents collect Vietnamese Dong and pay foreign currency to customers?
14/03/2026
No. Exchange agents are only allowed to collect foreign currency and pay out Vietnamese Dong to customers, in accordance with regulations.
Can exchange agents serve customers 24/7?
14/03/2026
Yes. However, the actual operating hours may vary depending on each agent’s service capacity.
Which customers can exchange foreign currency at authorized exchange agents?
14/03/2026
Authorized agents can exchange foreign currency for individual customers who are Vietnamese or foreign citizens wishing to convert foreign currency into Vietnamese Dong.

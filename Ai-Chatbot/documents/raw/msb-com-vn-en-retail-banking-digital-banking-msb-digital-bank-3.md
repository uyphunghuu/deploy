## Product information
Overview  Eligibility Criteria  Application Guide 
MSB Digital Bank – An international-standard financial super app: fast, powerful, multi-experience, seamlessly connecting every journey.
**Eligible Customers:**
  * Individual customers who are Vietnamese citizens
  * Customers aged 15 years and above


**Eligibility Requirements:**
  * Possess a valid mobile phone number
  * Register for and use the Mobile App in compliance with the Bank’s regulations
  * Agree to the Terms and Conditions governing the use of the Mobile App
  * Use a mobile device that meets the application’s technical requirements
  * Not fall under any cases subject to service restrictions or refusal in accordance with applicable laws and the Bank’s regulations


  * **Step 1** : Download the MSB Digital Bank app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  * **Step 2** : Open the app and select “Register for Digital Banking Services.”
  * **Step 3** : Enter your mobile phone number and complete OTP verification.
  * **Step 4** : Complete electronic Know Your Customer (eKYC) verification as instructed (Citizen ID card capture, chip scanning, and facial authentication).
  * **Step 5** : Provide additional personal information as required.
  * **Step 6** : Set up login credentials and authentication methods.
  * **Step 7** : Open an online payment account.
  * **Step 8** : Complete registration and start using the services.


## Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my Internet Banking / MSB mBank password. How can I retrieve it?
12/02/2026
To retrieve your Digital Banking password, please follow one of the options below:  
**1. Via the MSB mBank application:**
  * Step 1: Log in to the MSB mBank application and select “Forgot Password” on the login screen.
  * Step 2: Enter the required verification information, including: Username, Account Number, ID/Passport Number, and Date of Issue of the ID/Passport.
  * Step 3: The system will send a new password to the mobile phone number registered with MSB.


_**Note** : Password reissuance is only applicable on the mobile device that has previously successfully logged in to MSB Digital Banking services._
**2. Visit the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office for assistance**.
I forgot my username, how can I recover it?
12/02/2026
To have your Digital Banking username reissued, please follow one of the options below:
**Method 1: Contact MSB Customer Service via online channels**
You may request a username reissuance through MSB’s official channels:
  * **Zalo:** Ngân hàng Hàng Hải – MSB
  * **Facebook:** MSB – Ngân hàng TMCP Hàng Hải Việt Nam
  * **Email:** cskhcanhan@msb.com.vn


**Method 2:** Visit the nearest **MSB Branch/Transaction Office** for direct assistance.
Will my Digital Banking account be locked if I enter the wrong password multiple times?
12/02/2026
If you enter an incorrect login password more than **five (05) times** , your Digital Banking service will be temporarily locked to ensure account security.
In this case, you may unlock the service and request a password reissuance by following the steps below:
**Step 1: Unlock your Digital Banking service**
  * **Option 1: Contact the Customer Service Hotline**
    * Call **1900 6083** (service fee may apply).
    * After listening to the instructions, press **3** , then select **2** to receive automated assistance for unlocking the service.
  * **Option 2: Contact the MSB Customer Service Center via online channels**
    * **Zalo Official Account:** Ngân hàng Hàng Hải – MSB
    * **Facebook Fanpage:** MSB – Ngân hàng TMCP Hàng Hải Việt Nam
    * **Customer Service Email:** cskhcanhan@msb.com.vn
  * **Option 3: Visit the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office for assistance** * (Please bring your ID card or passport).


_**Note:** After your service has been successfully unlocked, please use the **“Forgot Password”** function and follow the instructions to reset your password._
**Step 2: Reissue your Digital Banking password**
  * **Option 1** : Via the MSB mBank application


  1.     1. Log in to the **MSB mBank** application and select **“Forgot Password”** on the login screen.
    2. Enter the required verification information, including: **Username, Account Number, ID/Passport Number, and Date of Issue of the ID/Passport.**
    3. The system will send a new password to the mobile phone number registered with MSB.


_**Note:** Password reissuance is only applicable on the mobile device that has previously successfully logged in to MSB Digital Banking services._
  * **Option 2** : Visit the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office for assistance

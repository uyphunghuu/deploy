## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
**The Fixed Asset Investment Loan at MSB** is a financing solution designed for customers investing in machinery, equipment, transport vehicles, or the construction and expansion of facilities serving production and business activities. MSB offers large loan limits, high financing ratios, and flexible principal grace periods, helping businesses secure capital and reduce financial pressure. With repayment methods aligned with cash flow and a transparent approval process, MSB supports businesses in realizing their investment plans and driving sustainable growth.
  * Aged from 20 to 75 years old.
  * Operating in business sectors compliant with applicable laws and regulations.
  * Stable and continuous business operations for at least 12 months.
  * A feasible funding plan, such as: Supplementing working capital, expanding production and business operations, and investing in fixed assets.


**Step 1: Register and submit your application online**
Applications can be submitted 100% online. An MSB specialist will contact you within 24 hours.
**Step 2: Document Submission**
  * ID Card/Citizen Identification Card and Household Registration Book (copies).
  * Collateral ownership certificate (copy).
  * Proof of income documents.
  * Construction contract/Purchase order/Payment request from the supplier.
  * Application assessment and verification.
  * Notification of loan approval results.


**Step 3: Signing of Credit and Security Agreements**
Including notarization, certification, and registration of collateral security.
**Step 4: Loan Disbursement**
– Loan approval within 16 working hours from the time the customer submits complete documentation.
– Funds are disbursed immediately after completion of collateral security registration procedures.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Which entities are eligible and suited for MSB’s Business Fixed Asset Investment Loan?
15/03/2026
The product is designed for individuals engaged in business activities, including self-employed individuals, household business representatives, or private enterprise owners.
What are the specific loan purposes?
15/03/2026
The loan may be used for business-related purposes, including:
  * Purchasing or transferring real estate/factories used as business premises.
  * Constructing or renovating real estate/factories used as business premises.
  * Purchasing machinery, equipment, or transport vehicles.
  * Refinancing loans for eligible business purposes.


What are the repayment methods?
15/03/2026
Customers pay interest monthly. The principal repayment is flexible, aligned with the customer’s cash flow and may be paid monthly or every 2–3–4 months.

# Attractive Incentives for Opening a Business Account at MSB in 2026
17/12/2025
To accompany businesses in their digital transformation journey and optimize operational costs, MSB officially launches a special incentive program for customers who open a new Business Account in 2026. With the motto “Connecting Success,” MSB provides comprehensive financial solutions along with superior digital experiences.
## 1. Outstanding Incentives for New Business Customers
When successfully opening a payment account at MSB, businesses will immediately enjoy a “Triple Incentive” package:
  * **100% Waiver of Transaction Fees:** Free domestic money transfers 24/7 via Internet Banking and Mobile Banking.
  * **Account Maintenance Fee Waiver:** No minimum balance requirement and no maintenance fees for the first 12 months.
  * **Preferential Loan Interest Rates:** Access to credit packages with interest rates reduced by up to **1.5% per annum** compared to the standard rate.

## 2. Popular Corporate Account Packages at MSB
Currently, MSB offers **two popular corporate account packages** for businesses:
### 2.1 M-Smart Standard Package – Free and Convenient
The **M-Smart Standard package** is a transaction account solution with diverse payment features. Businesses enjoy:
  * **100% free domestic and international online transfers**
  * **100% free registration and maintenance of digital banking services**
  * Convenient and flexible payment features that support daily business operations


This package is suitable for businesses looking for a **simple, efficient and cost-effective banking solution**.
### 2.2 M-Smart Plus Package – For Businesses Seeking Enhanced Benefits
The **M-Smart Plus account package** offers more advanced features, incentives and utilities for businesses with greater transaction needs.
This package is particularly suitable for:
  * Businesses involved in **import–export activities**
  * Companies with **high transaction frequency**
  * Enterprises with **significant foreign currency transaction needs**


With this package, businesses can enjoy enhanced financial services and optimized banking solutions tailored to their operational scale.

## 3. Corporate Account Opening Process at MSB
To help customers quickly receive incentives when opening a corporate account at MSB, the bank provides the following step-by-step guide:
**Step 1:** Access the website: <https://ebank.msb.com.vn/>  
**Step 2:** Select **“Corporate Customers”** and choose **“Open Online Payment Account.”**  
**Step 3:** Enter the enterprise’s **digital signature information**.  
**Step 4:** Complete all required company information, including corporate documents, general information updates, and identification documents of relevant individuals such as the **legal representative, authorized representative, chief accountant or accounting officer**.  
**Step 5:** Register for the **M-Banking service** with at least **three accounts** : administrator, approver and transaction executor according to the assigned roles.  
**Step 6:** Choose the **most convenient branch or transaction location**.  
**Step 7:** Review all registered information and then select **“Agree.”**  
**Step 8:** Authenticate using the **digital signature**. Customers will receive the **account number immediately** and can start performing necessary financial transactions.

## 4. Eligibility Conditions and Frequently Asked Questions about the Account Opening Offers
For the **M-Smart package** , the eligible customers who can receive incentives when opening a corporate account at MSB include:
  * Corporate customers nationwide who are not subject to exclusion cases and who open and use an **M-Smart current account at MSB for the first time** during the period from **January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025**.
  * Businesses nationwide that are not subject to exclusion cases, are existing MSB customers but **do not have a non-term account or have had no active transactions on their non-term account within the most recent 6 months** , and reopen and use the **M-Smart current account** during the period from **January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025**.
  * **Exclusion cases:** Banks, credit institutions, finance companies, financial leasing companies, securities companies, insurance companies, and fund management companies.


Currently, the **M-Smart corporate account opening promotion** is implemented continuously from **January 1, 2025 to May 30, 2026** , aiming to support businesses in expanding operations and improving financial efficiency.
When using promotional or cashback policies, please note the following:
  * Customers must conduct **online transactions in accordance with MSB’s specified conditions** to qualify for cashback.
  * The promotion program may change depending on bank policies and budget limits; **MSB reserves the right to adjust or terminate the program without prior notice**.
  * Customers must ensure that their accounts are **in good standing, not locked, and without transaction limit issues** when MSB processes cashback payments.
  * MSB may require customers to provide **relevant documentation** in case of disputes regarding cashback benefits and reserves the right to **reject transactions suspected of fraud or invalid transactions**.

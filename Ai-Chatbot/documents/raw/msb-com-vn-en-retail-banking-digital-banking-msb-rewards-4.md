## Product information
Overview  Eligibility criteria  Application Guide 
  * MSB Rewards is a loyalty program for MSB’s valued customers, including both individuals and businesses. 
  * By accumulating reward points through financial transactions, customers can easily redeem points for thousands of attractive rewards and exclusive offers.
  * From 24 December 2025, loyalty points earned from Super Free Credit Cards and Mastercard Hybrid Cards will be consolidated into M-Points on MSB Rewards, enabling customers to accumulate points faster and maximize the value of their rewards.


  1. **Eligible Customers:**


**1.1. Individual Customers:**
The Program applies to individual customers of MSB nationwide who meet the following conditions:
  * Classified as M-First, Upper Emerald, Emerald, or Standard customers in accordance with MSB’s regulations from time to time;
  * Have registered for and are using MSB’s Digital Banking services (excluding customers using the Inquiry-only Package);
  * Are using MSB’s products and services as specified in the Program Terms and Conditions attached hereto.


**1.2. Corporate Customers:**
The Program applies to all corporate customers of MSB nationwide who simultaneously meet the following conditions:
  * Identified in accordance with MSB’s regulations from time to time;
  * Have registered for and are using MSB Internet Banking (the Bank’s online banking service for corporate customers) (excluding customers using the Inquiry-only Package);
  * Are using MSB’s products and services as specified in the Program Terms and Conditions attached hereto. For the avoidance of doubt, customers participating in this promotional program are not customers under MSB’s Frequent Customer Program in accordance with MSB’s regulations/policies from time to time.


  1. **Applicable Conditions:**


  * Please refer to the detailed Terms and Conditions of the Loyalty Program for Individual Customers here 
  * Please refer to the detailed Terms and Conditions of the Loyalty Program for Corporate Customers [here](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/01/TnC-MSB-Reward-EB2.pdf)


  1. **Individual Customers**


Follow these simple steps to redeem rewards and enjoy thousands of attractive vouchers on MSB Rewards:
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/01/MSB.HD-do%CC%82%CC%89i-die%CC%82%CC%89m-MSB-Rewards.Eng-1-scaled.jpg)
**2. Corporate Customers**
Steps to follow:
  * **Step 1** : Access your MSB Internet Banking account and select MSB Rewards on the home screen;
  * **Step 2** : Choose categories based on your needs, such as Dining, Technology, Entertainment, etc;
  * **Step 3** : Review the offer details and select “Redeem Now” to receive the offer;
  * **Step 4** : Select “Use Now” for the desired voucher; the Checker approves the transaction to complete the voucher redemption.


## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Who can participate in the MSB Rewards program?
15/03/2026
All MSB individual customers and corporate customers are eligible to participate in the MSB Rewards program.
How can I earn reward points?
03/02/2026
  * Reward points are accumulated from transactions such as: QR payments, bill payments, online savings deposits, maintaining account balances, using debit cards, etc.
  * Spending via MSB Mastercard Super Free and MSB Mastercard Hybrid credit cards.
  * **Exclusive privileges for Priority Banking customers:** Rewards of up to 500,000 M-Points on their birthday


How can I redeem points for vouchers on the system?
03/02/2026
  * **Step 1:** Log in to the MSB mBank / MSB Digibank application.
  * **Step 2:** Select MSB Rewards.
  * **Step 3:** Choose your preferred voucher.
  * **Step 4:** Select “Redeem Now” to use the voucher.

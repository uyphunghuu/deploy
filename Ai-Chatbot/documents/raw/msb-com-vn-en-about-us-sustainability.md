## Our Commitment
MSB is committed to **placing the interests of all stakeholders first** , including investors, customers, employees, and the community. With a long-term vision, MSB focuses on delivering tangible value, contributing to sustainable socio-economic development, protecting the environment, and enhancing quality of life for society.  
We are **committed to harmonizing economic interests with social value and environmental protection.**
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/03/muctieu_moi-truong.png)
### Environmental Objective
Paving the way for sustainable transition
As a responsible financial institution, MSB is committed to advancing sustainable finance and driving a comprehensive transition across its ecosystem, thereby strengthening the resilience of both the Bank and its stakeholders in addressing the challenges of climate change.
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/03/muctieu_xahoi.png)
### Social Objective
Sharing human-centered value
Placing people at the center of every decision, advancing financial inclusion, and partnering with communities throughout their journey toward sustainable development.
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/03/muctieu_qua%CC%89n-tri%CC%A3.png)
### Governance Objective
Breakthroughs for the future
Upholding governance principles grounded in compliance and transparency, accelerating digital transformation, and laying a strong foundation for sustainable economic growth.

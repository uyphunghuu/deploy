## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
**The New Car Loan at MSB** provides a flexible financing solution for customers looking to own a new car. With preferential interest rates fixed during the first year, long loan tenures, and a high financing ratio, MSB helps customers realize their car ownership plans while reducing financial pressure and maintaining balanced spending. A simple application process and dedicated advisory support throughout the journey make purchasing your new car easier and more convenient.
  * Aged from 20 to 65 years old.
  * Loan purpose: purchasing a new car, or refinancing funds used to purchase a new car for personal travel needs of the customer or their spouse, parents, siblings, or children.
  * Collateral required: the financed vehicle, valuable papers, or real estate may be used as collateral.
  * The vehicle must: Have a usage period of less than 5 years, have fewer than 9 seats, have a minimum market value of VND 200 million, and not originate from China, Taiwan, or India.


**Step 1: Loan Application Registration**
An MSB specialist will contact you within 24 hours after receiving your application.
**Step 2: Document Submission**
  * ID Card/Citizen Identification Card and Household Registration Book (copies).
  * Collateral ownership certificate (copy).
  * Proof of income documents.
  * Vehicle purchase documents, down payment receipt or third-party loan documents.
  * Vehicle registration certificate or appointment letter for vehicle registration.


**Step 3: Signing of Credit and Security Agreements**
Including notarization, certification, and registration of collateral security.
**Step 4: Loan Disbursement**
The loan will be approved within 16 working hours once all required documents are submitted. Funds will be disbursed immediately after completion of the collateral security registration procedures.

#### 2015
### Community & Sustainability 2015
The “Community & Sustainability” award presented by International Data Group (IDG) and the Vietnam Banks Association is a source of great pride, recognizing MSB’s ongoing commitment to social responsibility.
Beyond annual financial contributions, MSB has supported community housing, empowered underprivileged students, and provided timely assistance to pediatric patients. Through its “Community Bank” model, MSB has also enabled thousands of small traders and farmers to access capital and improve livelihoods.
For MSB, sustainable development goes hand in hand with shared prosperity for customers and society.

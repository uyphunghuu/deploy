Kredivo is a technical platform that provides financial solutions for users, financial partners, commercial establishments, etc. With the Kredivo platform, licensed credit institutions (Kredivo's financial partners) provide financial products such as Buy Now Pay Later, cash loans, etc. to serve the shopping and consumption needs of individual customers. With the Buy Now Pay Later product on the Kredivo platform, buying in installments without a credit card is just a small matter. You can enjoy shopping at Kredivo's partners such as FPTShop, CellphoneS, Dien May Cho Lon, Viettel Stores, Hoang Ha mobile, Con Cung .... and choose to pay with financial products provided by licensed credit institutions through the Kredivo platform easily, conveniently and quickly. With the Kredivo platform, you can also enjoy loans from financial partners to pay for essential daily services such as phone top-up, utility bill payment, etc. Conditions to apply for a loan from financial partners: - You must be a Vietnamese citizen with a valid ID card/CCCD - At least 18 years old - Residing in Vietnam - Having a minimum income of 4,000,000 VND per month It only takes 5 minutes to register with Kredivo. All you need to provide is your ID card/CCCD, portrait photo, and verified address. Your profile will be approved in just a few hours. Once your profile is approved, you can start enjoying Kredivo's services right away. What are you waiting for? Download Kredivo now to enjoy the best financial services for your daily spending needs!

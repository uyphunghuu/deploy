## Product information
Overview  Eligibility criteria  Application Guide   
| **General Overview**  | A savings product that allows customers to receive the full interest amount upfront at the time of deposit, while the principal is paid at maturity.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Tenor**  | 1-13 months, 15, 18, 24, and 36 months  |  
| **Minimum Deposit Amount**  | No minimum amount required  |  
| **Interest Payment Method**  | One-time interest payment, paid upfront at the time of deposit  |  
| **Early Withdrawal**  | Customers are allowed to withdraw before maturity and will earn non-term deposit interest rates. The system will automatically recalculate the interest earned and deduct from the principal any excess interest already paid beyond the interest actually entitled.  |  
| **Maturity**  | If the customer does not close the deposit on the maturity date, the principal will not be automatically renewed and will instead earn non-term deposit interest for the period from maturity onward. If the maturity date falls on a weekend or public holiday as prescribed by MSB, the payment of principal and interest will be processed on the next business day.  |  
| **Eligible Applicants**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Application Requirements**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| **How to Open an Upfront Interest Savings Account at Branches**  | Step 1: Prepare required documents: Bring your original, valid identification documents: chip-based Citizen ID / Level-2 electronic Citizen ID / Passport. Step 2: Visit an MSB branch: Go to the nearest branch or transaction office of MSB. Step 3: Request and complete the application: Inform the bank staff of your request to open an Upfront Interest Savings account. The staff will provide and guide you through the required application form. Step 4: Deposit funds and receive confirmation: Make the deposit and receive a physical savings passbook or confirmation via the banking app.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **How to Open an Upfront Interest Savings Account Online**  | Step 1: Log in to the MSB Digital Bank app on your mobile device. Step 2: Select “Deposits & Investments.” Step 3: Choose “Open a New Term Deposit” and select the deposit product that suits your needs. Step 4: Enter the required information: – Source account – Deposit amount (subject to any minimum deposit requirement) – Deposit tenor (e.g. 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, etc.) and applicable interest rate – Maturity instructions and other options (if any) Step 5: Tick “Agree” to the Terms & Conditions to proceed. Step 6: Review the details and authenticate the transaction to open the deposit. Step 7: The system will notify you of a successful trans  |

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Can customers withdraw funds before maturity multiple times during the savings period?
18/03/2026
Yes. With the **Partial Withdrawal Savings** product, customers can flexibly withdraw funds before maturity multiple times:
  * **Up to 5 withdrawals** for savings accounts opened **at branches**
  * **Unlimited withdrawals** for **online** savings accounts


What is the enhanced security layer for savings accounts?
12/02/2026
Customers will have an additional protection layer when closing online savings accounts through security questions. Customers may select up to 3 security questions. When enabling or disabling the feature, customers must correctly answer the security questions set at the time of service registration.
Which MSB deposit product currently offers the highest interest rate?
03/02/2026
MSB’s **Highest Interest Rate Savings** product currently offers **a competitive interest rate of up to 5.8%** , helping customers maximize returns on their idle funds.

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#corporate-banking)
Can I borrow in USD, as my imports are settled in USD?
12/03/2026
Yes. USD-denominated loans may be granted provided that the enterprise has import and export activities in compliance with applicable foreign exchange regulations.
Besides unsecured financing, can I obtain financing secured by collateral?
15/03/2026
Yes. Businesses may obtain loans secured by collateral, with financing value of up to 280% of the appraised collateral value
What is the approval and disbursement timeline?
12/03/2026
  * Express credit facilities based on the enterprise’s reputation: up to VND 15 billion, approved within 3 working days.
  * Comprehensive credit facilities of up to VND 200 billion: approval within 10 working days.

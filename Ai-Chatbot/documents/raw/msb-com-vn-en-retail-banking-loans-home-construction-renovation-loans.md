## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
**The Home Construction & Renovation Loans at MSB** offer flexible financing solutions for customers planning to build, renovate, or upgrade their homes. With **a preferential interest rate fixed at 6.0% per year for the first 6 months, large loan limits, and a fast approval process with streamlined procedures** , MSB helps customers proactively plan their finances and implement construction projects smoothly. MSB’s advisory team provides dedicated support throughout the process, assisting with documentation at your location to ensure a convenient and reassuring borrowing experience while building your ideal home.
  * Aged from 20 to 70 years old.
  * Minimum monthly income: VND 5 million.
  * Loan purpose: financing for home construction or renovation for yourself or family members, for residential or rental purposes; including refinancing funds already used for construction/renovation or settling outstanding construction-related payments.
  * Collateral required: valuable papers or real estate may be used as collateral.


**Step 1: Register and submit your application online**
Applications can be submitted 100% online. An MSB specialist will contact you within 24 hours.
**Step 2: Document Submission**
  * ID Card/Citizen Identification Card and Household Registration Book (copies).
  * Collateral ownership certificate (copy).
  * Proof of income documents.
  * Construction contract/Purchase order/Payment request from the supplier.


**Step 3: Application Assessment and Approval Notification**
**Step 4: Signing of Credit and Security Agreements**
Including notarization, certification, and registration of collateral security.
**Step 5: Loan Disbursement**
Funds will be disbursed immediately after completion of the collateral security registration procedures.

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
What fees should I prepare before disbursement?
15/03/2026
Customers are required to pay the collateral valuation fee, based on which MSB determines the loan amount according to the value of the collateral. This fee is paid when submitting the loan application documents to the Bank. Once the loan is approved, customers must prepare the notarization fee for the security agreement and the collateral security registration fee to complete the mortgage procedures before disbursement.
What is the monthly repayment amount?
16/03/2026
Loan repayment refers to the principal and interest amounts that the customer must pay for the loan.
What is the equal principal, reducing balance repayment method?
15/03/2026
This is a repayment method in which the principal is paid in equal installments monthly or every 2–3 months, while interest is calculated based on the outstanding principal balance.

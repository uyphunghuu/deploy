## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
**The Consumer Overdraft at MSB** is:
  * A flexible financing solution that allows you to spend first and settle later, helping meet daily financial needs quickly and conveniently.
  * With an approved overdraft limit, you can continue making payments even when your account balance reaches zero. This ensures stable cash flow and gives you greater confidence in managing everyday expenses while maintaining effective financial control.


  * Aged from 20 to 70 years old.
  * Collateral required: real estate owned by the customer or a third party (guarantor).
  * Flexible proof of income.
  * A feasible funding plan for personal consumption and living expenses.


**Step 1: Register and submit your application online**
Applications can be submitted 100% online. An MSB specialist will contact you within 24 hours.
**Step 2: Document Submission**
  * ID Card/Citizen Identification Card and Household Registration Book (copies).
  * Collateral ownership certificate (copy).
  * Proof of income documents.
  * Construction contract/Purchase order/Payment request from the supplier.


**Step 3: Application Assessment**
The loan application will be assessed and the approval result will be notified to the customer.
**Step 4: Signing of Credit and Security Agreements**
Including notarization, certification, and registration of collateral security.
**Step 5: Loan Disbursement**
  * The loan will be approved within 16 working hours from the time the customer submits complete documentation.
  * Funds will be disbursed immediately after completion of the collateral security registration procedures.

## Frequently Asked Questions

What is the monthly repayment amount?
16/03/2026
Loan repayment refers to the principal and interest amounts that the customer must pay for the loan.
What types of assets are accepted as collateral for the business loan?
16/03/2026
Customers may use the following assets as collateral for the loan: valuable papers, savings books, deposit balances, real estate, or transport vehicles.
Can MSB provide support if I do not have a business registration certificate
16/03/2026
MSB does not require a business registration certificate if the customer’s business activities are not legally required to register for a business license.
If the customer’s business is legally required to register but has not yet obtained a business registration certificate and/or relevant operating licenses, the customer must provide at least one of the following documents:
  * Tax payment receipts or other tax/fee payment documents.
  * Confirmation from the business management authority (e.g., market management board, shopping mall management, or local People’s Committee).
  * A written commitment to provide the business registration certificate within 6 months.


For customers conducting online businesses, proof of business activities such as orders on e-commerce platforms or bank account statements is sufficient. MSB may still provide financing of up to VND 1 billion.

Google Pay™ is a secure, fast, and convenient digital payment method developed by Google on the Google Wallet™ app, exclusively for mobile devices running the Android operating system. Google Pay™ allows MSB cardholders to make one-touch payments with only their mobile device, without carrying cash or swiping card as traditional payment methods.
Google Pay™ is available for all types of MSB Visa/Mastercard international cards. Add your card to Google Wallet™ to start experiencing the service now!

  * Condition: Customers must add an MSB Master/Visa credit or debit card to Google Pay and complete a successful, eligible payment of at least 100,000 VND via Google Pay

## Frequently Asked Questions
Why should I use Google Pay?
08/05/2026
Google Pay helps you make payments safely, quickly, and conveniently, so you don’t have to waste time looking for your cards or carrying cash in wallet. Simply use your Android device, and you can complete your transaction instantly with just one touch.
Is my device compatible with Google Pay?
08/05/2026
Android device on version 9.0 or higher, that has NFC/HCE technology, is compatible with Google Pay.
Is it safe to use Google Pay for payment?
08/05/2026
Google Pay offers a safer payment method compared to using a traditional physical card. Your actual card number is encrypted during transactions via a tokenized number, and your transaction requires two-factor authentication through Google’s security.

| **Overview**  | Critical Illness Insurance is a supplementary insurance product that provides long-term financial support for the treatment of various critical illnesses at different stages. It offers peace of mind for you and your loved ones while helping you confidently move toward a healthier and more secure future.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Entry age**  | 16 – 55 years old (renewable continuously up to age 65)  |  
| **Maximum age at policy maturity**  | 65 years old  |  
| **Policy term**  | 1 year  |  
| **Premium payment term**  | Annually  |  
| **Waiting period**  | ● First heart attack, first stroke: 90 days ● Cancer: Thyroid cancer (early & advanced stages): 365 days; Other cancers: 90 days ● Survival period: The insured must survive at least 7 days after diagnosis.  |  
100% of the Sum Insured is payable for: early-stage cancer, advanced-stage cancer, first heart attack, and first stroke.

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
If I already have health insurance or a healthcare card, will I still receive benefits if I purchase Critical Illness Insurance?
26/03/2026
Yes. Benefits from Critical Illness Insurance are paid independently of Health Insurance or Healthcare Insurance.
Does Critical Illness Insurance reimburse actual hospital expenses?
26/03/2026
No. Critical Illness Insurance provides a lump-sum payment based on the medical diagnosis, and 100% of the Sum Insured is paid according to the policy benefits.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Is physiotherapy treatment covered by insurance?
27/03/2026
Yes. Insurance covers physiotherapy treatment expenses when the treatment is prescribed and deemed medically necessary by a doctor. The reimbursement ranges **from VND 50,000 to VND 100,000 per day** , up to **60 days per year** , applicable to **Gold, Platinum, and Diamond plans**.
How many days after policy expiry can customers renew the insurance policy?
27/03/2026
Customers who purchase insurance through MSB may **renew their policy within a maximum of 7 days** from the expiry date and still be considered a continuous renewal (no waiting period applied). However, the renewed insurance certificate will only take effect from the date the renewal premium is paid.
If a customer has two health insurance policies, how will the insurance benefits be paid?
27/03/2026
If a customer has two valid health insurance policies, GIC **will reimburse medical treatment expenses within the coverage scope of the policy**. The total reimbursement amount **will not exceed either the actual medical invoice or the combined benefit limit of the two policies** , whichever is lower.

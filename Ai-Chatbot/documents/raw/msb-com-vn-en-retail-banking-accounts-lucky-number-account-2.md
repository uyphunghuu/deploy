## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn more ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
How is a Lucky Number Account different from a regular current account?
03/02/2026
A Lucky Number Account is still a current account and provides full banking services, including deposits, withdrawals, payments, transfers, and other banking utilities.
How many Lucky number accounts can I open?
11/03/2026
You may open an **unlimited number of Lucky Number accounts**. However, the M-Lucky account number based on your date of birth can only be opened once.
I already have an MSB account. How can I open an additional lucky number account?
12/02/2026
You can easily register for an additional MSB Lucky Number Account through the following methods:
### **1. Via MSB mBank App**
  * **Step 1:** Log in to the **MSB mBank** app, select **“Accounts,”** and then choose **“Open M-Lucky Account.”**
  * **Step 2:** Select one of the three available lucky number categories:
    * **M-Lucky:** Personalized based on your date of birth.
    * **Feng Shui:** Numbers aligned with auspicious meanings.
    * **Customized:** Choose any sequence according to your preference.
  * **Step 3:** Select your desired account type, follow the on-screen instructions, and confirm your successful registration.


### **2. At the Branch**
  * Simply visit your nearest **MSB branch or transaction office** , and our staff will be happy to assist you with the opening process.

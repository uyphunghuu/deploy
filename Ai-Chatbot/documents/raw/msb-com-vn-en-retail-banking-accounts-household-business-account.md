**I. Current Account for Household Businesses at MSB**
The Current Account for Household Businesses is a financial solution designed by MSB specifically for owners of online and traditional retail businesses, supporting efficient revenue and cash flow management. By opening an account with MSB, household businesses can access all standard Current Account features, conveniently receive payments via store QR codes, and centrally manage and track revenues on the MSB mBank digital banking application. This helps optimize operations and enhance transparency in financial management.
**II. Benefits of Opening an MSB Current Account for Household Businesses**
Opening a Current Account with MSB enables household businesses to manage their finances conveniently and securely on a digital banking platform, improve cash inflows and outflows through cashless payments, and access preferential offers and financial solutions tailored to business needs. Through this, MSB supports household businesses in optimizing cash flow, improving capital efficiency, and establishing a solid foundation for stable and sustainable business operations.
**Eligible Applicants:** Household businesses and individual business owners in accordance with applicable laws and regulations
**Application Requirements:** Full submission of household business registration documents in accordance with MSB’s regulations
Account Opening at Bank Branches / Transaction Offices
  * Step 1: Prepare all required documents and visit the nearest MSB branch or transaction office.
  * Step 2: Present valid identification documents and request to open a Current Account optimized for household businesses.
  * Step 3: Complete the account opening application form as guided by the bank staff.
  * Step 4: MSB staff review and verify the documents. Upon successful verification, the account will be opened and guidance on using the digital banking application will be provided.
  * Step 5: Complete the registration and start using the account.

Can I change the default account used for transfers?
12/02/2026
Yes, you can change the default account for transfers.  
To change the default account, please follow these steps:
  * **Step 1** : Log in to the MSB mBank application.
  * **Step 2** : Select Settings → Payment Settings.
  * **Step 3** : Choose Change Default Account and follow the instructions.


Can I choose which savings accounts to apply the enhanced security layer to?
12/02/2026
Yes. Customers may select one or multiple online savings accounts (up to 10 accounts per request).
Are there any fees for opening an account?
11/03/2026
Not at all. Account registration is completely free of charge.

## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide   
|   
 | Overview  | The SWIFT international transfer service enables customers to send money abroad through the global SWIFT payment network. The system connects thousands of banks worldwide, allowing cross-border transfers to be processed quickly and accurately.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| Key Features  | 
  * Enables customers to transfer funds internationally to bank accounts in many countries around the world.
  * Transactions are processed through a global banking network with clear and transparent transfer information, making it easy to trace when necessary.

 |  
| Currencies  | Foreign currencies announced by MSB and quoted in the bank’s daily exchange rate schedule.  |  
| Transfer Limits  | Limits are applied depending on the purpose of the customer’s transaction.  |  
| Processing Time  | Funds are typically credited to the beneficiary’s account within 1-3 business days, depending on the receiving bank and destination country.  |  
 |  
| Eligible Customers  | Vietnamese citizens or foreign individuals legally residing and operating in Vietnam who wish to transfer money to overseas individuals or organizations through a bank.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| Conditions  | Individual customers who need to transfer money abroad for lawful purposes in accordance with applicable regulations.  |  
|   
 | How to Make an International Transfer via SWIFT at a Branch  | Step 1: Visit the nearest MSB branch to initiate the transaction. Step 2: An MSB teller will advise on the appropriate transfer purpose based on the customer’s needs. Step 3: Provide the required transfer details, identification documents, supporting documents for the transfer purpose, and beneficiary information. Step 4: MSB verifies the validity of the information, transfer amount, and source of funds. Step 5: The customer receives a transfer receipt/confirmation from MSB.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| How to Make an International Transfer via SWIFT Online  | Step 1: Log in to the MSB mobile application. Step 2: On the home screen, select “Services” and then choose “International Transfer.” Step 3: Read the transfer instructions and confirm “I understand.” Step 4: Create a transfer profile
  * Select “Transfer Profile” and choose “Create Profile.”
  * Select the desired transfer purpose and enter the required information.
  * Complete the profile and wait for MSB approval.

Step 5: MSB verifies the validity of the information, transfer amount, and source of funds and approves the profile. Step 6: Create a transfer order
  * Go to “Transfers”, select “Create Transfer Order,” and choose the valid profile.
  * Click “Create Transfer Order,” select transaction fee, and confirm the exchange rate.
  * Submit the transfer order to MSB.

Step 7: MSB processes the transaction according to the customer’s request. Step 8: The customer receives the transfer receipt/confirmation via email.  |

Do I need to pay any fees when transferring money via the SWIFT channel?
13/03/2026
When making a transfer via the SWIFT channel, customers are required to pay the following fees:
  * Transfer fee
  * SWIFT/telegraphic fee
  * Foreign bank charges


What beneficiary information is required when making an international transfer via SWIFT?
13/03/2026
When making an international transfer via SWIFT, customers need to provide the following information:
  * Beneficiary bank information: bank name, address, SWIFT code, and country of the beneficiary bank.
  * Beneficiary information: full name, account number or IBAN, and address.


What information do I need to provide to someone overseas so they can transfer money to Vietnam?
14/03/2026
  * Provide the following beneficiary account information in Vietnam: 
    * Bank Information 
      * Beneficiary Bank: Vietnam Maritime Commercial Joint Stock Bank
      * SWIFT Code: MCOBVNVX
      * Bank Address: 54A Nguyen Chi Thanh Street, Lang Ward, Hanoi, Vietnam
  * Beneficiary Account Information at MSB: Full name, Account number, Address

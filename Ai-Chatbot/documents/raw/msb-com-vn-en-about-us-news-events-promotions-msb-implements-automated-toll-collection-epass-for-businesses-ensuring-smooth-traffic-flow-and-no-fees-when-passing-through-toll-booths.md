# MSB implements automated toll collection (ePass) for businesses – ensuring smooth traffic flow and no fees when passing through toll booths.
26/12/2025
To support businesses in proactively complying with regulations and optimizing operations, Vietnam Maritime Commercial Bank (MSB) has pioneered a partnership with Vietnam Digital Transport Joint Stock Company (ePass) to officially launch the ePass automatic debit service for corporate customers.
![](https://axkujhcqrnim.compat.objectstorage.ap-singapore-1.oraclecloud.com/msbweb-bucket/msb-website/2026/03/dcc3c8c0-2153-bbd6-f699-4c1e159d29d6.png)
According to government regulations, by December 31, 2025, vehicles passing through ETC toll stations will be required to use a traffic account linked to cashless payment methods.
In order to support businesses in proactively complying with regulations and optimizing operational activities, the Commercial Joint Stock Bank Vietnam Maritime Bank (MSB) is a pioneer in cooperating with Vietnam Digital Transport Joint Stock Company (ePass) to officially launch the ePass automatic debit service for corporate customers.
**Payment of tolls – Automatic & Uninterrupted**
With this service, businesses enjoy many practical benefits:
• No need for manual top-up, reducing the risk of insufficient balance at toll booths
• Automatic payment of tolls via payment account
• Reduces operational pressure for accounting and fleet management departments
**Simple registration in just 5 minutes – Peace of mind operation**
To use the service, businesses only need to:
1. **For businesses without an MSB account:** Open an account online**[here](https://digibank.msb.com.vn/business/account/register/login)**.

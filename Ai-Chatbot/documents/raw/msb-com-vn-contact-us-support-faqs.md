## Frequently Asked Questions
Career  Corporate Banking  Financial Institutions  Priority Banking  Retail Banking 
Beyond the base salary, what are MSB’s welfare policies and development roadmaps?
14/03/2026
In addition to competitive KPI-based bonuses, MSB offers premium health insurance, team-building activities, and particularly, transparent promotion roadmaps. We encourage internal rotation to allow employees to maximize their potential, ensuring your career is always moving and evolving.
What sets MSB’s working environment apart from other banks?
14/03/2026
MSB takes pride in its **Digital-first environment** and **Agile culture**.
**Highlights:** Modern workspace, minimal hierarchical barriers, encouraging creativity from the staff level. This is a place where you not only work but also “upgrade” yourself through major projects and the bank’s vibrant engagement activities.
What are the core criteria MSB uses to evaluate candidates?
14/03/2026
MSB prioritizes candidates with a “Customer-Centric” mindset and the ability to adapt quickly to digital technology. We look for colleagues who demonstrate high responsibility, proactivity, and alignment with our 4 core values: **Responsibility – Efficiency – Innovation – Customer-Centricity.**
If I don’t have experience in the banking sector, does MSB provide training programs for newcomers?
14/03/2026
At MSB, we don’t just look for experience; we value potential. Every new employee undergoes a structured “Onboarding” program and specialized courses at MSB. In particular, you will be guided by experienced “Buddies” to quickly acclimate to business processes, products, as well as the culture and environment at MSB.
Additionally, MSB offers “tailor-made” programs for Freshers, such as **MAT (Management Associate Trainee)** or **RM Start-up**.
**Key Highlights:**
  * **Comprehensive Training Roadmap:** Candidates participate in 2 weeks of intensive theoretical training at the Head Office, followed by 2 months of hands-on field experience at business units.
  * **Superior Benefits:** MSB provides full salary starting from the theoretical training phase – a competitive privilege reserved for high-potential personnel.
  * **Sustainable Development Foundation:** The roadmap is designed to help candidates adapt step-by-step, understand corporate culture, and master professional procedures before officially taking on their roles.


How long does the recruitment process at MSB usually take and how many rounds are involved?
14/03/2026
MSB aims for efficiency and speed. Typically, the process takes **about 2-3 weeks** , consisting of 3 steps: **Application screening, Competency assessment/Preliminary interview,** and**In-depth interview**. Results are provided promptly to help candidates remain proactive with their personal plans.

## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide   
| **General Overview**  | A savings product that allows customers to receive monthly interest payments throughout the deposit period.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Tenor**  | 2-13 months, 15, 18, 24, and 36 months  |  
| **Currency**  | VND  |  
| **Interest Payment Method**  | Monthly interest payments  |  
| **Early Withdrawal**  | Customers are allowed to withdraw before maturity and will earn non-term deposit interest rates. The system will automatically recalculate the interest earned and deduct from the principal any excess interest already paid beyond the interest actually entitled.  |  
| **Maturity**  | If the customer does not close the deposit on the maturity date and no other agreement is in place, MSB will automatically roll over both principal and interest into a new corresponding tenor at the interest rate applicable at the time of renewal. (If the maturity date falls on a weekend or public holiday as prescribed by MSB, the payment of principal and interest will be processed on the next business day.)  |  
| **Eligible Applicants**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Application Requirements**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| **How to Open a Recurring Interest Savings Account at Branches**  |  **1. Prepare required documents** : Bring your original, valid identification documents: chip-based Citizen ID / Level-2 electronic Citizen ID / Passport. **2. Visit an MSB branch** : Go to the nearest branch or transaction office of MSB. **3. Request and complete the application** : Inform the bank staff of your request to open a Recurring Interest Savings account. The staff will provide and guide you through the required application form. **4. Deposit funds and receive confirmation** : Make the deposit and receive a physical savings passbook or confirmation via the banking app.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **How to Open a Term Deposit Online**  |  1. Log in to the MSB Digital Bank app on your mobile device. 2. Select “Deposits & Investments.” 3. Choose “Open a New Term Deposit” and select the deposit product that suits your needs. 4. Enter the required information:
  * Source account
  * Deposit amount (subject to the minimum deposit requirement)
  * Deposit tenor (e.g. 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, etc.) and applicable interest rate
  * Maturity instructions and other options (if any)

5. Tick “Agree” to the Terms & Conditions to proceed. 6. Review the details and authenticate the transaction to open the deposit. 7. The system will notify you of a successful transaction. You can monitor your savings account directly in the app.  |

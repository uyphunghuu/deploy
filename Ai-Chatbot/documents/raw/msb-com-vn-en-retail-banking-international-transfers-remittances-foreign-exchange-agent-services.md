## Product information
Product Information  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide  Additional Information   
|   
 | Overview  | The Foreign Exchange Agent network is established through cooperation with economic organizations that meet the conditions stipulated by the State Bank of Vietnam.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| Key Features  | ● Provides cash foreign currency exchange services into Vietnamese Dong for individuals in need. ● Implemented at the locations of economic organizations that have signed agency agreements with MSB and have been licensed by the State Bank of Vietnam.  |  
| Currencies  | Common foreign currencies such as USD, EUR, JPY, AUD, SGD, GBP, etc.  |  
| Exchange Rate  | Exchange rates are listed and updated continuously according to MSB’s official rate schedule, ensuring transparency and accuracy.  |  
 |  
| Eligible Applicants  | Economic organizations that meet the conditions stipulated by the State Bank of Vietnam.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| Conditions  | Economic organizations must satisfy the eligibility requirements under regulations of the State Bank of Vietnam.  |  
| How to Register as an MSB Foreign Exchange Agent  | Step 1: Sign an agency agreement with an MSB branch. Step 2: Obtain approval from the State Bank of Vietnam. Step 3: Conduct staff training in accordance with MSB’s regulations. Step 4: Commence business operations.  |

## Frequently Asked Questions

Can I receive a remittance sent via RIA if I do not have an MSB account?
14/03/2026
Yes. You can visit the nearest MSB transaction location to receive the money without having an MSB account.
Do I have to wait long when making an international transfer via RIA?
13/03/2026
Once customers provide **complete and valid documentation** , the transaction will be processed within **approximately 20–30 minutes** , and the recipient can receive the money shortly after.
Do I have to pay any fees when receiving remittances via RIA at MSB?
14/03/2026
No. Customers are not required to pay any fees when receiving remittances via RIA at MSB.

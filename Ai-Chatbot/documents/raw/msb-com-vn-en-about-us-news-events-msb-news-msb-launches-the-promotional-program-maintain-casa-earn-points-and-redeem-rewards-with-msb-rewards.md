# MSB launches the promotional program “Maintain Casa – Earn points and redeem rewards with MSB Rewards”
10/11/2025
MSB officially launches a special promotional program: “Maintain Casa – Earn points and redeem gifts with MSB Rewards”(*), to express gratitude to the Corporate Customer segment that has trusted and used MSB’s products and services.
![](https://axkujhcqrnim.compat.objectstorage.ap-singapore-1.oraclecloud.com/msbweb-bucket/msb-website/2026/03/dc58d930-b1eb-9ff6-e233-5f372bc5d7b9.jpg)  
MSB officially launches a special Promotion Program: “Maintain Casa – Earn Points and Redeem Gifts with MSB Rewards”(*), to express gratitude to the Corporate Customer segment that has trusted and used MSB’s products and services.
This program is a heartfelt thank you, and also provides an opportunity to accumulate attractive reward points, helping you optimize capital utilization and increase financial benefits.
  1. GENERAL INFORMATION AND APPLICATION PERIOD APPLICATIONS 
     *        * **Promotion Name:** “Maintain Casa – Earn Points and Redeem Gifts with MSB Rewards”
       * **Application Period:** From now until December 31, 2025
       * **Scope:** Nationwide. 
         *            1. TARGET AUDIENCE AND PARTICIPATION CONDITIONS
2.1 Target Audience
     *        *          1.            1.               * SSE Corporate Customers – i.e., micro-enterprises as defined by MSB from time to time, and new customers at MSB for 60 days or more, maintaining an average balance in their payment account and not yet having applied the monthly customer care policy;
              * RBTC Corporate Customers – i.e. are a group of medium-sized enterprises & Small or large as defined by MSB at each period, maintaining an average balance in the payment account and not yet having been included in the monthly customer care policy;2.2 Participation ConditionsCorporate customers who have registered and used MSB’s Internet Banking service (online banking service for corporate customers) (not applicable to customers using the Inquiry Package). 
Customers using a successfully opened corporate payment account at MSB (CASA).
3
PRIZE STRUCTURE: EXTREMELY ATTRACTIVE M-POINT REWARDS
Promotional goods are MSB M-points, with an exchange rate of 1 M-point = 1 VND. With accumulated M-point rewards, your company can redeem attractive e-vouchers from an ecosystem of over 1,000 reputable brands through the MSB Rewards section on MSB’s Internet Banking platform.
3.1. Special Offers for RBTC Corporate Customers  
|  **Average Balance Growth Value** **(Net CASA Increase)**  | **Reward Points (M-point)**  |  
| --- | --- |  
| For every 1 billion VND or more  | 100,000  |  
3.2. Special Offer for SSE Corporate Customers
From 300 million VND to under 500 million VND
100,000  
|  **Average Balance Growth Value** **(Net CASA Increase)**  | **M-point Rewards**  |  
| --- | --- |  
| From 50 million VND to under 300 million VND  | 15,000  |  
| From 500 million VND to less than 1 billion VND  | 160,000  |  
| From 1 billion VND onwards  | 500,000  |

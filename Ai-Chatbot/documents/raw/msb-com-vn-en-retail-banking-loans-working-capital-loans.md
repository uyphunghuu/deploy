## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
**The Working Capital Loan at MSB** is a flexible financing solution designed to meet funding needs for production and business operations. With large loan limits, convenient eligibility requirements, and repayment plans aligned with cash flow, MSB enables customers to secure the capital needed to maintain and expand their business activities. This product is suitable for various business models, including customers operating online businesses via social media or e-commerce platforms. With a transparent approval process and dedicated advisory support, MSB provides effective financing solutions to help businesses operate stably and sustainably.
  * Aged from 20 to 70 years, and not exceeding 75 years old at loan maturity.
  * Borrowers may apply as individuals (business owners, household business owners, spouses of business owners, or capital contributors) or as legal entities (micro enterprises).
  * Operating in business sectors compliant with applicable laws and MSB regulations.
  * At least 1 year of business experience, or 2 years for online loan applications.


**Step 1: Loan Application Registration**
Complete the consultation form and an MSB specialist will contact you for assistance.
**Step 2: Document Submission**
  * ID Card/Citizen Identification Card and Household Registration Book (copies).
  * Documents evidencing business activities.
  * Proof of income documents.
  * Loan proposal documents (e.g., business plan).


**Step 3: Loan Disbursement**
  * Loan approval within 24 working hours (applicable for online loan applications).
  * Flexible disbursement options: online or at the branch.

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Which entities should opt for MSB’s Working Capital Loan?
15/03/2026
The product is designed for individuals engaged in business activities, including self-employed individuals, household business representatives, or private enterprise owners.
What lending structures does MSB offer for working capital loans?
15/03/2026
MSB offers flexible lending structures, including short-term single-disbursement loans, credit limit facilities, and medium- to long-term loans, tailored to each customer’s business needs.
What is the maximum tenor of the loan?
15/03/2026
The maximum loan tenor is up to **36 months**. MSB will review the approved credit limit **every 12 months** to ensure it remains aligned with the customer’s business performance.

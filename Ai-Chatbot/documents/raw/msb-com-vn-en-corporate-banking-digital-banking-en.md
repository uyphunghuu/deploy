Can customers utilize the approved framework credit limit immediately after approval?
13/05/2026
This depends on the customer segment eligible for the framework credit limit. 
  * Priority Customers: Eligible for immediate utilization of sight L/C and bank guarantee limits (excluding payment guarantees and loan guarantees).
  * Standard Customers: Credit limits may be utilized in line with the collateral supplementation schedule.


What are the financing purposes of this product?
13/05/2026
Working capital financing and/or fixed asset investment.
What financing solutions are available under this product?
13/05/2026
Short-term loans, medium- and long-term loans, overdraft facilities, credit cards, factoring, guarantees, Letters of Credit (L/C), export document discounting, and negotiation of export documents under L/Cs.

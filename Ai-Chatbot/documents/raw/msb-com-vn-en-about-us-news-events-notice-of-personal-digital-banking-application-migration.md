# Retail digital banking application migration notice
15/05/2026
# **ELEVATE YOUR DIGITAL EXPERIENCE WITH THE SUPER APP – MSB DIGITAL BANK**
Welcome to **MSB Digital Bank** – a Super App tailored to personalize every financial journey, empowering customers to master their cash flow and enjoy a more convenient lifestyle.
From June 1, 2026, MSB officially launches the new digital banking platform, MSB Digital Bank, and initiates support for customers transitioning from the legacy application MSB mBank to the new MSB Digital Bank app. Detailed migration information will be communicated via MSB’s official channels to all customers.

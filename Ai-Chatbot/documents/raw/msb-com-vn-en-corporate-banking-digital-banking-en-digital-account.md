## Product information
Product Information  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
Opening an MSB business account via eKYC is a 100% online process, allowing businesses to complete account opening procedures within minutes without visiting a branch. Businesses can select a premium account number, enjoy free transfers, and manage their accounts 24/7 through the MSB Business Banking digital platform. 
**100% ONLINE**
  * **No need to submit documents** at the counter.
  * Account activated**within a few hours** with transaction limits of up to **VND 20 billion per day.**


**OUTSTANDING BENEFITS**
  * **Free premium account numbers** with attractive feng shui interpretation; preferential**M-Smart package.**
  * **Instant** digital certificate issuance, **free** for the first year.


**OPTIMIZED & FLEXIBLE JOURNEY**
  * **Capture documents directly** from mobile devices and automatically upload to the browser.
  * Fast **digital signing** of documents **within seconds.**
  * Process **navigation menu** allowing flexible movement between steps.
  * Customers can view and manage submitted applications, **track progress** and application status.


Applicable to all corporate and institutional customers operating in accordance with Vietnamese law (resident and non-resident entities such as legal entities, enterprises, business households, and other organizations), meeting the following conditions:
  * Legally established, resident, and operating in Vietnam
  * Not listed in any sanctions or AML screening lists under which MSB declines to establish relationships
  * No U.S. indicia and not subject to FATCA compliance requirements

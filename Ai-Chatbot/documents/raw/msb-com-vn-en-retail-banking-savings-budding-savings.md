## Product information
Overview  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide   
| **General Overview**  | A savings product designed for children, opened and managed by parents or legal guardians, helping build long-term financial savings for the child’s future.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Tenor**  | 3-13 months, 15, 18, 24, 36 months, and 4-15 years  |  
| **Currency**  | VND, USD  |  
| **Minimum Deposit Amount**  | 
  * Initial deposit: VND 1,000,000 or USD 100
  * Subsequent periodic contributions: VND 50,000 or USD 2

 |  
| **Interest Payment Method**  | At maturity  |  
| **Interest Rate**  | Subject to MSB’s regulations from time to time  |  
| **Early Withdrawal**  | Customers are allowed to withdraw before maturity and will earn non-term deposit interest rates.  |  
| **Eligible Applicants**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Application Requirements**  | Vietnamese citizens aged 18 and above.  |  
**How to Open a Budding Savings Account at Branches:**
  1. **Prepare required documents** : Bring your original, valid identification documents: chip-based Citizen ID / Level-2 electronic Citizen ID / Passport.
  2. **Visit an MSB branch** : Go to the nearest branch or transaction office of MSB.
  3. **Request and complete the application** : Inform the bank staff of your request to open a Budding Savings account. The staff will provide and guide you through the required application form.
  4. **Deposit funds and receive confirmation** : Make the deposit and receive a physical savings passbook or confirmation

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn More ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Can customers authorize another person to close their savings account?
03/02/2026
Yes. MSB allows customers to **authorize another person to close a savings account opened at a branch** , in accordance with the bank’s current regulations.
Can customers withdraw funds before maturity multiple times during the savings period?
18/03/2026
Yes. With the **Partial Withdrawal Savings** product, customers can flexibly withdraw funds before maturity multiple times:
  * **Up to 5 withdrawals** for savings accounts opened **at branches**
  * **Unlimited withdrawals** for **online** savings accounts


What is the enhanced security layer for savings accounts?
12/02/2026
Customers will have an additional protection layer when closing online savings accounts through security questions. Customers may select up to 3 security questions. When enabling or disabling the feature, customers must correctly answer the security questions set at the time of service registration.

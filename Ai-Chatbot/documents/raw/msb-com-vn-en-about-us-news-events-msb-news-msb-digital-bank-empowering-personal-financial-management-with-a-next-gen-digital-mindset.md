# MSB Digital Bank: Empowering Personal Financial Management with a Next-Gen Digital Mindset
02/06/2026
**_By integrating a multi-layered security technology solution, optimizing the interface based on customer behavior, and expanding the utility ecosystem, the MSB Digital Bank application aims to elevate the digital financial experience for each individual customer._**
MSB Digital Bank is a core milestone in MSB’s 5-year comprehensive digital transformation strategy, while also marking the bank’s 35-year journey of establishment (August 12, 1991 – August 12, 2026). This application simultaneously establishes new operational standards for the digital financial market based on infrastructure and user experience platforms.
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/06/msb-digital-bank.jpg)
Starting from June 1st, MSB officially implements customer support to transition from the old MSB mBank application to the new MSB Digital Bank application.
## Proactive Multi-Layered Security Architecture
The MSB Digital Bank application is developed on the Backbase system — a digital banking architecture solution adopted by many international financial institutions to ensure stable operational performance and high-speed processing of large volumes of data. In digital banking risk management, protecting the information security and assets of customers is the top control priority. Therefore, MSB Digital Bank is equipped with the latest international standard security technologies that will help establish a proactive, robust, and solid technological defense system.
At the same time, MSB Digital Bank also integrates complementary technological solutions, including: FIDO transaction authentication, Face Authentication, and the Bshield solution to protect mobile applications on both iOS and Android operating systems. In addition, coordinating operations with technology partners such as ePay, Trusting Social, and connecting data from the Ministry of Public Security helps optimize the electronic Know Your Customer (eKYC) process. This mechanism not only shortens transaction processing times but also enhances control capabilities, preventing unauthorized interventions into the system and ensuring seamless continuity even under fluctuating network conditions.
## Service Personalization Based on Financial Behavior
The next advancement in the structure of MSB Digital Bank is the ability to optimize the interface and features based on user-centric design thinking, eliminating fixed mass-display models. Concurrently, the interface display structure can flexibly change according to customer segments (priority customers and mass customers).
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/06/nguoi-dung-co-the-lua-chon-hinh-nen.jpg)
Users can select a display background from a pre-available list in the MSB Digital Bank gallery.
According to an MSB representative, the MSB Digital Bank application was developed with the goal of becoming a smart personal financial assistant, aiming to elevate the experience to accompany users in all aspects of modern life. Through analyzing demographic data and historical financial behavior, the system automatically recognizes and proactively proposes products, features, and promotional policies tailored to each individual’s needs. This approach streamlines operational processes, transforming the application into a financial assistant that supports users in making quick decisions.
## “All-in-One” Utility Ecosystem
From a foundation of customer understanding, the application expands its service scope through online connections with third-party partner ecosystems. The goal is to fully meet financial and consumer needs on a single interface.
![](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/06/khach-hang-co-the-thuc-hien-dau-cuoi-.jpg)
Customers can perform end-to-end tasks: from opening accounts online, ultra-fast card opening, and purchasing online insurance to participating in the Marketplace service ecosystem and the MSB Rewards loyalty program. Notably, the 100% automatically approved credit solution allows customers to experience a combo of 3 products: Loans, credit cards, and overdrafts with online disbursement limits of up to VND 1 billion along with attractive interest rates.
## Optimizing Cash Flow Management Performance
With the objective of providing tools to help customers optimize their finances from available funds, MSB Digital Bank integrates various smart financial solutions, allowing idle money in accounts to achieve high and continuous yield rates. Besides, to ensure liquidity, users can flexibly withdraw money 24/7 when unexpected spending needs arise without disrupting the cash flow monetization journey on their payment accounts. This factor helps customers proactively manage their finances and grow their assets on personal digital financial applications.
The deployment of MSB Digital Bank based on international technological standards, maintaining seamless performance even under fluctuating network infrastructure conditions, reflects MSB’s commitment to an in-depth digital transformation strategy. Instead of focusing on isolated features, MSB prioritizes synchronization between system safety and actual response capability. This is the foundation to drive business model transformation, shaping the role of accompanying customers to elevate their digital financial experience.
MSB will support customers in transitioning from the old MSB mBank application to the new MSB Digital Bank application starting from June 1st. Detailed information will be notified to each customer through official channels.
For details on the application transition guide, please view [**here**](https://www.msb.com.vn/ve-chung-toi/tin-tuc-va-su-kien/thong-bao-chuyen-doi-ung-dung-ngan-hang-dien-tu-khach-hang-ca-nhan/) or contact Hotline 1900 6083.

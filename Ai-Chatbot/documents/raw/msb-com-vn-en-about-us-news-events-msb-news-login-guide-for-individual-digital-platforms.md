# Login guide for individual digital platforms
18/05/2026
## LOGIN TO INDIVIDUAL DIGITAL PLATFORMS
Please select the digital platform you are currently using to continue:
![](https://digibank-onboarding-uat.msb.com.vn/casabucket/msb-website/uat/2026/05/2aOboQXFaN8PDQsGUAqsLzWM5yzIHZlA6WDixCAS-1-scaled.jpg)
Starting from 01/06/2026, MSB officially launches the new digital banking platform, MSB Digital Bank, and provides support for customers to migrate from the existing application.
For customers who have successfully migrated to the new MSB Digital Bank platform, please:
  *     * **Login** : [Here](https://digibank-identity-retail.msb.com.vn/auth/realms/customer/login-actions/authenticate?client_id=bb-web-client&tab_id=zCHbQi_hkdU&execution=8c10f557-9ac7-4140-9148-f9cfe1014374&kc_locale=en)


_**OR**_
![](https://digibank-onboarding-uat.msb.com.vn/casabucket/msb-website/uat/2026/05/2aOboQXFaNEMyJzszjhufDzc0fvXXKqJP9YagenI-1-scaled.jpg)
For customers currently using the MSB mBank digital platform. You will be invited to migrate to the new MSB Digital Bank platform in the near future. Please continue using MSB mBank until you receive the migration notification.
  * **Login**[here](https://ebank.msb.com.vn/IBSRetail/Request?&dse_sessionId=QJwEUtom___PaymV6A-_Ym8&dse_applicationId=-1&dse_pageId=1&dse_operationName=retailIndexProc&dse_errorPage=error_page.jsp&dse_processorState=initial&dse_nextEventName=start)

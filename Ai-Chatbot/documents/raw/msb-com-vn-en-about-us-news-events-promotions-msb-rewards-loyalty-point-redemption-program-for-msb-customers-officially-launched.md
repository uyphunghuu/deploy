# MSB Rewards: Loyalty Point Redemption Program for MSB Customers Officially Launched
24/02/2025
**Starting from February 24, 2025, MSB Rewards** – MSB’s **point accrual and gift redemption program** has officially launched. This program honors our loyal customers, allowing you to accumulate reward points from banking transactions and redeem them for thousands of attractive gifts.
**What makes MSB Rewards special?  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) Easy Point Accrual:** Receive M-Points immediately when performing financial transactions with MSB.  
**![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg)Attractive Redemptions:** Thousands of gifts from reputable partners, including dining, fashion & beauty, technology, travel, and entertainment vouchers…  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) **Level Up – More Benefits:** The higher your membership tier, the more points you accumulate to redeem gifts from MSB.  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg)**Convenient Experience:** Check M-Points and redeem gifts quickly via MSB mBank.
**![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd5mTca7j-qWCVA2dYEUVfdh2WQOBsf5Nxw8--XUb2KB4RqD6Q8rXGYNeSjm5aV129WQgOoGoq-Ijy-Cr1eAuAJ_mfoG69_YSNK4FhgmIpbWfhJB49niLfyDA_5SY4vfKkwezN58A?key=aHKOa2HerRqHx1RI9Q7VCHb2)**
**Earn Points Easily – Receive Attractive Gifts**
Transactions at MSB that help you earn **M-Points** include:
**![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg)Maintaining payment account balances.  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) Online bill payments & mobile top-ups.  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) Online savings deposits.  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) QR code payments.  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) Payments with MSB debit cards.  
![🔹](https://s.w.org/images/core/emoji/17.0.2/svg/1f539.svg) M-Point birthday gifts.**
(And more point-earning transactions will be updated in the near future)
**Simple Redemption – Thousands of Gifts**
You can use reward points to redeem shopping vouchers, service discounts, or direct cashback to your account. MSB Rewards partners include BeBike, Grab, iSushi, Gogi, The Coffee House, etc., and many other premium brands.
**How to Redeem Gifts**
Step 1: Log in to MSB mBank/ MSB Digibank app
Step 2: Select MSB Rewards
Step 3: Choose your voucher
**Eligible Customers**
The program is open to individual MSB customers nationwide who meet the following conditions:
(i) Identified as M-First, Upper Emerald, Emerald, or Standard customers according to MSB’s regulations from time to time;
(ii) Registered for and using MSB’s Digital Banking services (does not apply to customers using the Inquiry Package);
(iii) Utilizing MSB products and services as specified in the attached program terms and conditions.
**Program Terms and Conditions**

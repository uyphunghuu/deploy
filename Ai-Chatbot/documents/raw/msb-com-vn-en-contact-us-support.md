How to update Click to Pay registration information?
03/06/2026
To update your Email or Phone number, please contact our hotline at 1900 6083 for support.
Is there any fee for using Click to Pay?
03/06/2026
You will not be charged any additional fees when making one-click payments via Click to Pay.
Where can I use Click to Pay?
03/06/2026
Use Click to Pay at checkout pages that support this feature:
  * Available on all platforms – websites, mobile apps, and more.
  * Works on any device – phone, tablet, or laptop.


_Note: Only cards enrolled in Click to Pay can be used._
Is Click to Pay secure?
03/06/2026
Yes. Click to Pay uses advanced tokenization technology to protect your information. You can complete payments in just one click without re-entering your card details, enjoying a faster and more convenient checkout experience while keeping your personal and card information highly secure when shopping online.
How does Click to Pay work?
03/06/2026
When shopping on websites that support Click to Pay, you can complete your payment in just one click by entering the phone number or email registered with your enrolled card.
  * Step 1: Select Click to Pay as your payment method
  * Step 2: The system will display the list of credit cards linked with Click to Pay
  * Step 3: Choose the card you want to use
  * Step 4: Confirm your transaction


_Note: Card details are not transmitted. Instead, they are encrypted and replaced with a secure token to ensure fast, convenient, and safe payment processing._
Can a guarantee be released by cash payment or by documentary settlement?
12/03/2026
Yes. The release of guarantees may be effected by cash payment or documentary settlement, depending on the customer’s business plan and subject to the Bank’s approval.
If an enterprise applies for secured financing, what is the maximum financing ratio?
12/03/2026
Financing of up to 280% of the collateral value may be granted, subject to asset valuation and the Bank’s approval.
What financing products are available under this solution?
12/03/2026
MSB provides comprehensive financing packages, including: Loans, Issuance of bank guarantees, Letters of Credit (L/C)
What is the current interest rate for enterprises trading in metal products?
12/03/2026
The applicable interest rate for each enterprise depends on the Bank’s internal credit rating, the loan amount, and the banking services utilized. For detailed consultation on the interest rate applicable to your enterprise, please leave your contact information and the Bank will contact you upon receipt.
My revenue last year was VND 50 billion, and it is projected to reach VND 100 billion this year. How much can I borrow?
12/03/2026
The loan amount is determined based on the customer’s actual funding needs, debt repayment capacity, and financial management capability, subject to the Bank’s credit assessment and approval.
In what form will customers receive gifts when participating in Prudential promotional programs through the MSB channel?
20/03/2026
  * For electronic voucher gifts, the voucher will be sent by the gift provider Got It / Urbox (Prudential’s partners) to the phone number or email address registered by the customer with Prudential.
  * For physical gifts, Prudential or the gift provider will deliver the gift to the customer via postal service with direct delivery, based on the phone number and Citizen ID card information provided by the customer.
  * Customers must provide accurate phone number and contact address information to Prudential when completing the information at the time of purchasing the insurance.


How can customers learn about life insurance promotion programs currently available at MSB?
20/03/2026
Promotional programs for Prudential life insurance distributed through the MSB channel are typically publicly posted on the MSB website and the Prudential website. Customers may contact the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office or their relationship manager to receive full and detailed consultation.
What is the term deposit interest rate for MSB Priority Banking customers?
15/03/2026
Priority Banking customers who place online term deposits receive an additional 0.5% interest rate compared to regular customers, and an additional 0.2% interest rate for deposits made at the counter. In addition, customers are exempt from early termination fees within 2 working days from the deposit opening date for counter transactions.
What is the non-term deposit interest rate for MSB Priority Banking customers?
15/03/2026
MSB Priority Banking customers are entitled to non-term deposit interest rates of up to 0.5% per year, depending on the end-of-day balance in the current account.
What credit card installment programs does MSB currently offer?
15/03/2026
MSB offers a variety of installment options to meet customers’ needs:
  * 0% interest installment: MSB credit cards support 0% installment payments at major partners such as Shopee, Nguyễn Kim, Samsung, OceanEdu, CellphoneS, and many other partners.
  * Flexible installment for any transaction: Convert any transaction into an installment plan with interest rates starting from 1.5% per month, with a tenor of up to 24 months.
  * Outstanding balance installment: Convert transactions that have already appeared on the statement into installment payments, helping reduce financial pressure with interest rates starting from 1.5% per month, with installment terms of up to 36 months.


What are the key contents of a repurchase agreement?
14/03/2026
A repurchase agreement shall include the following principal contents:
  1. Seller;
  2. Buyer;
  3. Details of the valuable papers: form (certificate or book-entry), serial number, par value or maturity value (total amount payable at maturity), issuing organization, maturity date;
  4. Purchase date;
  5. Purchase price;
  6. Purchase interest rate;
  7. Purchase term;
  8. Repurchase price;
  9. Repurchase date;
  10. Method of payment and transfer of valuable papers;
  11. Dispute resolution and breach handling;
  12. Rights and obligations of the parties;
  13. Value date of the agreement;
  14. Other relevant terms and conditions.


Which foreign currencies are supported for domestic transfers at MSB?

MSB supports up to 12 major currencies, including: USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, JPY, NOK, DKK, AUD, CAD, SGD, HKD, and THB.
What is the limit per transaction for 24/7 Interbank Transfers?
15/03/2026
The transaction limit for 24/7 Interbank Transfers is up to nearly 500 million VND per transaction.
How can I adjust my transaction details?
15/03/2026
**You can submit a trace request to adjust the account number or beneficiary name for successful transfers from MSB. Please follow these steps:**
  * **Step 1:** Log in to Internet Banking → Select **Accounts** → Choose the account used for the transaction.
  * **Step 2:** Select **Transaction History** and find the transaction you wish to trace.
  * **Step 3:** Click **Trace** → Enter your adjustment request in the “Content” field.


**Note:** Trace requests are processed within a maximum of 04 working days. Feedback will be sent via the user’s registered phone number.
Who should I contact for support if a transaction error occurs over the weekend?
15/03/2026
For every Collection & Payment client, MSB provides a dedicated on-site support team. Additionally, a 24/7 communication channel is maintained with our IT experts and product specialists to ensure all issues are resolved instantly.
Beyond the base salary, what are MSB’s welfare policies and development roadmaps?
14/03/2026
In addition to competitive KPI-based bonuses, MSB offers premium health insurance, team-building activities, and particularly, transparent promotion roadmaps. We encourage internal rotation to allow employees to maximize their potential, ensuring your career is always moving and evolving.
What sets MSB’s working environment apart from other banks?
14/03/2026
MSB takes pride in its **Digital-first environment** and **Agile culture**.
**Highlights:** Modern workspace, minimal hierarchical barriers, encouraging creativity from the staff level. This is a place where you not only work but also “upgrade” yourself through major projects and the bank’s vibrant engagement activities.
What are the core criteria MSB uses to evaluate candidates?
14/03/2026
MSB prioritizes candidates with a “Customer-Centric” mindset and the ability to adapt quickly to digital technology. We look for colleagues who demonstrate high responsibility, proactivity, and alignment with our 4 core values: **Responsibility – Efficiency – Innovation – Customer-Centricity.**
If I don’t have experience in the banking sector, does MSB provide training programs for newcomers?
14/03/2026
At MSB, we don’t just look for experience; we value potential. Every new employee undergoes a structured “Onboarding” program and specialized courses at MSB. In particular, you will be guided by experienced “Buddies” to quickly acclimate to business processes, products, as well as the culture and environment at MSB.
Additionally, MSB offers “tailor-made” programs for Freshers, such as **MAT (Management Associate Trainee)** or **RM Start-up**.
**Key Highlights:**
  * **Comprehensive Training Roadmap:** Candidates participate in 2 weeks of intensive theoretical training at the Head Office, followed by 2 months of hands-on field experience at business units.
  * **Superior Benefits:** MSB provides full salary starting from the theoretical training phase – a competitive privilege reserved for high-potential personnel.
  * **Sustainable Development Foundation:** The roadmap is designed to help candidates adapt step-by-step, understand corporate culture, and master professional procedures before officially taking on their roles.


How long does the recruitment process at MSB usually take and how many rounds are involved?
14/03/2026
MSB aims for efficiency and speed. Typically, the process takes **about 2-3 weeks** , consisting of 3 steps: **Application screening, Competency assessment/Preliminary interview,** and**In-depth interview**. Results are provided promptly to help candidates remain proactive with their personal plans.

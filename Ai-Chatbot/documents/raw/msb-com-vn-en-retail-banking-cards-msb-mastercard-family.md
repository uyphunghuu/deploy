How can I apply for an MSB credit card?
12/02/2026
To apply for an MSB credit card, customers must meet the following requirements:  
1. Nationality: Vietnamese citizen  
2. Residence/Workplace: Located in provinces or cities where MSB Branches/Transaction Offices are available  
3. Age: Primary cardholder: From 20 to 65 years old; Supplementary cardholder: From 15 years old and above  
4. Credit history: No Group 2 bad debt within the last 12 months; No Group 3 bad debt within the last 24 months  
5. Income (salary transferred via bank account): From VND 6 million per month or more; From VND 8 million per month or more for customers in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City; The applicant must not work in hazardous or high-risk occupations (Income requirements may vary depending on MSB policies and specific card products from time to time.)  
6. Other requirements: According to MSB regulations applicable from time to time  
Note: Please visit https://taichinhtieudung.msb.com.vn or go to the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office to apply for an international credit card. The credit assessment team will review the application and provide feedback to the customer.
How can I add my MSB card to Apple Pay?
12/02/2026
You can link your MSB card to Apple Pay using one of the following methods:  
**1. Add the card via the MSB mBank application**  
Step 1: Log in to the MSB mBank application → Select Card Services → Choose Add Card to Apple Pay → Select Continue.  
Step 2: Select the card you wish to add to Apple Pay.  
Step 3: Select Add to Apple Wallet, then tap Next and Confirm to accept the Terms and Conditions.  
Once completed, your card will be successfully added to Apple Pay.  
**2. Add the card directly via Apple Wallet**  
Step 1: Open the Apple Wallet application on your device.  
Step 2: Tap the Add (+) button to start the process of adding a card to Apple Pay.  
Step 3: Follow the instructions to complete the setup. After the process is completed, your MSB card will be successfully linked to Apple Pay and ready for payment transactions.
How can I check my credit card statement and outstanding balance?
12/02/2026
You can check the monthly credit card statement and outstanding balance through the following methods:
  * **Method 1: Via the electronic statement sent by email**


You may review your credit card statement through the electronic statement sent to your registered email address.
  * **Method 2: Via the MSB Plus application**


Log in to the MSB Plus application → Select Card Services → Choose Statement → Select the month you want to review. Note: On the MSB Plus application, you can view credit card statements for the most recent 6 months.

Overview  Insurance Benefits  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide  Other Information   
| Overview  | 
  * Pru-Linked Insurance – Steady Investment Growth is designed for customers seeking a reliable insurance and investment solution.
  * Seven PRUlink investment funds with different risk levels provide flexible options, enabling customers to select an investment strategy that matches their financial goals and risk appetite.

 |  
| --- | --- |  
| Entry age  | 30 days to 70 years old.  |  
| Maximum age at policy maturity  | 100 years old.  |  
| Policy term  | 100 minus entry age.  |  
| Premium payment term  |  Equal to the policy term. Flexible premium payments are available from the 5th policy year.  |  
| Death Benefit  | 100% of the Sum Assured + Basic Account Value + Top-Up Account Value (if any).  |  
| --- | --- |  
| Total and Permanent Disability (TPD) Benefit  |  1. TPD benefit not caused by early-stage thyroid cancer: 100% of the Sum Assured + Basic Account Value + Top-Up Account Value (if any). 2. TPD benefit caused by early-stage thyroid cancer: The lower of: (a) 10% of the Sum Assured, or (b) VND 100 million. This benefit is payable only once during the policy term.  |  
| Investment Benefit  | Opportunity to invest and grow assets through seven PRUlink funds managed by Eastspring Investments Ltd.  |  
| Maturity Benefit  | Upon policy maturity, the customer will receive 100% of the policy account value, after deduction of any outstanding liabilities (if any), provided that the policy remains in force.  |  
| Bonuses to Increase the Policy Account Value  |  1. Growth Bonus: Bonuses of up to 300% of the first policy year’s annualized basic premium, payable as follows:
  * 10th policy anniversary: 150% of the first policy year’s annualized basic premium.
  * 15th policy anniversary: 75% of the first policy year’s annualized basic premium.
  * 20th policy anniversary: 75% of the first policy year’s annualized basic premium.

2. Loyalty Bonus: Bonuses calculated as a percentage of total deducted risk charges, payable as follows:
  * 10th policy anniversary: 50% of total risk charges deducted during the first 10 policy years.
  * 15th policy anniversary: 25% of total risk charges deducted from policy year 11 to year 15.
  * 20th policy anniversary: 25% of total risk charges deducted from policy year 16 to year 20.

 |  
| Transfer of Accumulated Value to a New Insurance Policy  |  The Policyholder may transfer the accumulated policy value to a new insurance policy with a new insured person when: The current insured reaches 60 years of age, or from the 20th policy anniversary, whichever occurs later. Key features of the new policy include:
  * The entire policy account value will be transferred to the new policy.
  * No mandatory premium payment period.
  * No initial charges.

 |  
| Flexible Features  | 
  * Flexibility to adjust the Sum Assured according to protection needs at different life stages.
  * Option to increase the Top-Up Account Value, with additional investments of up to five times the annualized basic premium of the current policy year.
  * Ability to switch funds (2) and adjust investment allocation among the seven PRUlink funds without fees.
  * Flexible premium payment from the 5th policy year.
  * Partial withdrawals from the policy account value (3) without fees to meet financial needs.
  * Ability to add optional riders for enhanced protection.

What is the Pru – Steady Investment Growth product?
27/03/2026
Pru – Steady Investment Growth is a life insurance product under the Pru-Linked Insurance (unit-linked insurance) category. Policy benefits are clearly separated between: Risk protection benefits (applicable in cases of death and/or total and permanent disability), and investment benefits
Insurance premiums will be allocated into unit-linked funds, which are invested in financial markets. The Policyholder receives the actual investment returns and bears the associated investment risks.
What benefits does Pru – Steady Investment Growth provide?
27/03/2026
The product provides the following benefits:
  * Risk protection benefits: protection against death and/or total and permanent disability.
  * Investment benefits: premiums are allocated into Prudential-managed unit-linked funds, and customers receive the full investment results while bearing the associated investment risks.
  * Maturity benefit: receive 100% of the policy account value.
  * Account value bonus benefits:


  *     * Growth Bonus
    * Loyalty Bonus


In which cases will the death benefit not be paid?
27/03/2026
Prudential will not pay the death benefit if the insured dies due to any of the following causes, whether directly or indirectly:
  * Suicide or self-inflicted injury within two years from the date the first basic premium is paid or the most recent policy reinstatement date.
  * Criminal acts or intentional misconduct by the Policyholder causing harm to the insured.
  * Criminal acts or intentional misconduct by the Beneficiary causing harm to the insured.
  * The insured being executed under a court judgment.
  * The insured being infected with HIV/AIDS or HIV-related illnesses (except for certain special cases under applicable regulations).
  * The insured using narcotics, illegal stimulants, or substances not prescribed by a physician.

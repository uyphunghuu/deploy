Who can participate in the MSB Rewards program?
18/03/2026
All MSB individual customers and corporate customers are eligible to participate in the MSB Rewards program.
How are M-Points in the MSB Rewards program used?
15/03/2026
M-Points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned. Customers can access the MSB Digibank application, select MSB Rewards, tap on the available M-Points, then select Explore Vouchers and choose categories that suit their needs such as dining, fashion, beauty, technology, travel, entertainment, and more to redeem.

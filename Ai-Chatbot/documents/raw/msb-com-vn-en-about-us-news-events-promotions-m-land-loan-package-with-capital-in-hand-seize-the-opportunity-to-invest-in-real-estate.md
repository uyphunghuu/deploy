# M-Land Loan Package – With capital in hand, seize the opportunity to invest in real estate.
09/01/2026
Loan limits up to 50 billion VND!
![](https://axkujhcqrnim.compat.objectstorage.ap-singapore-1.oraclecloud.com/msbweb-bucket/msb-website/2026/03/62b03588-0862-1b9c-6e2f-82fad6d460f6.jpg)  
In real estate investment, timing is the key factor determining profit. Even a slight delay, when capital hasn’t yet shifted, can cause business opportunities to slip away. Understanding the market’s “peak,” MSB introduces the M-Land loan package – a flexible real estate business loan solution designed to help investors proactively manage their capital and be ready to act as soon as opportunities arise.
**1. M-Land Loan Package – Real Estate Business Loan Solution**
**1.1. Optimal Loan Program for Investors**
– Key advantages of the M-Land loan package: Flexible credit limits based on experience and income from real estate transactions & other sources of income.
**– Competitive loan conditions, suitable for actual investment needs:**
  * Loan limit up to 50 billion VND.
  * Principal grace period up to 60 months.
  * Financing up to 90% of capital needs.
  * Flexible income verification documents, quick and easy procedures.


**– Flexible income verification:**
  * Income from the sale/transfer of existing real estate.
  * Income from the sale of existing real estate to repay principal at the end of the term.


**1.2. Purpose of borrowing**
– Purchase/acquisition of real estate.
– Compensation for the purchase/transfer of real estate.
**1.3. Priority Privileges:**
  * Receive a credit card with a limit of up to 1 billion VND.
  * Grant an unsecured overdraft with a limit of up to 500 million VND.


**2. Who is the M-Land loan package for?**
– Professional real estate investors
– Regularly involved in buying, selling, and investing in real estate.  
– Need a flexible capital solution to capitalize on market trends.  
– Desire to optimize cash flow turnover and improve investment efficiency.
– Business owners/household businesses investing in real estate
– Real estate investors aiming for growth and asset accumulation.
– Need a stable source of capital suitable for long-term investment plans.
– Prioritize quick procedures and loan conditions that closely match business realities.
– Proactive capital management – ​​Ready to seize opportunities Seize every investment opportunity!
When the time is right, MSB provides capital, helping real estate investors stay proactive and ready to implement their business plans as soon as new opportunities arise in the market.

# May promotions with MSB and Prudential “May of Love – Peace of Mind for Mother”
04/05/2026
period of application 05/05/2026 - 15/05/2026  (Expired) 
Dear valued customers,
Give the gift of health so that Mother can always live with peace of mind and joy every day. MSB in collaboration with Prudential introduces a special FLASH SALE program for May: “MAY OF LOVE – PEACE OF MIND FOR MOTHER”.
  1. Promotion period: from 05/05/2026 to the end of 31/05/2026


  * Insurance application forms submitted from 05/05/2026 to the end of 15/05/2026
  * Insurance policies issued from 05/05/2026 to 31/05/202


  1. Applicable products:


  * All products except PRU – AN TÂM BẢO TÍN


  1. Promotional gifts:


  * Got It E-voucher worth 1,500,000 VND for the first premium period corresponding to 01 valid Policy with a value from 50 million VND or more
  * Got It E-voucher worth 500,000 VND for the first premium period corresponding to 01 valid Policy with a value from 20 million VND to less than 50 million VND For details about the promotion program, [see here](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/05/BIBP_BC_0098.MSB-Q22026-May-Flash-Promotion-05.05.2026-15.05.2026.pdf).


Customers please contact hotline: 1900 6083 or visit the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office for free consultation support.

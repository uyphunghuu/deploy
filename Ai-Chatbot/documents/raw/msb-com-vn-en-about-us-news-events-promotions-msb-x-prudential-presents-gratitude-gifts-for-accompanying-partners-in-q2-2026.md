# MSB x Prudential presents gratitude gifts for accompanying partners in Q2.2026
16/04/2026
period of application 03/04/2026 - 31/05/2026  (Expired) 
Dear valued customers,
MSB understands that peace of mind does not come from a momentary decision, but is built from persistent choices over time. Therefore, renewing insurance is a simple way for customers to continue maintaining peace of mind for themselves and their families.
In this Q2, MSB would like to express gratitude to customers who have trusted and accompanied Prudential Insurance products through the program **“Gratitude gifts for accompanying partners Q2.2026 – Phase 1”**.
**1. Promotion period:** From April 3, 2026, to the end of May 31, 2026.
**2. Applicable products:**
  * All products except for PRU – AN TÂM BẢO TÍN.


**3. Promotional gifts:**
  * **Got It E-voucher worth 2,500,000 VND** for the actual periodic insurance premium or actual insurance premium to successfully restore the validity of 01 valid Contract from 60 million VND or more.
  * **Got It E-voucher worth 1,500,000 VND** for the actual periodic insurance premium or actual insurance premium to successfully restore the validity of 01 valid Contract from 40 million VND to less than 60 million VND.
  * **Got It E-voucher worth 500,000 VND** for the actual periodic insurance premium or actual insurance premium to successfully restore the validity of 01 valid Contract from 20 million VND to less than 40 million VND.


For program details, please see [[here]](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/04/BIBP_BC_0095.MSB-Q22026-Renewal-Promotion-Batch-1-03.04.2026-31.05.2026.pdf).
Please contact the hotline: 1900 6083 or visit the nearest MSB Branch/Transaction Office for free consultancy support.

### Criteria
## M-First Membership
Begin your journey of refined experiences with us.
Clients are required to meet one of the following conditions:
  * Average total assets over the past 90 days: 1 billion VND or more
  * Average credit outstanding over the past 90 days: 5 billion VND or more
  * Average non-term deposit balance over the past 90 days: 150 million VND or more
  * Total life insurance contract value: 100 million VND – under 300 million VND
  * Cumulative outgoing international transfers over the past 12 months: 3 billion VND or more

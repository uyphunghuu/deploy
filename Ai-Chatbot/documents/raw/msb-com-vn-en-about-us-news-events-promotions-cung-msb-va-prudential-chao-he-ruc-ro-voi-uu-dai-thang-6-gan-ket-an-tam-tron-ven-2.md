# Join MSB and Prudential to Welcome a Brilliant Summer with the Promotion “June Connection – Complete Peace of Mind”
01/06/2026
period of application 06/06/2026 - 30/06/2026  (Ongoing) 
Dear Valued Customers,
To welcome a brilliant summer, MSB in cooperation with Prudential brings you a special, exclusive FLASH SALE promotion only for this June: **“JUNE CONNECTION – COMPLETE PEACE OF MIND”**.
###  **1. Promotion Period:** From June 6, 2026, to the end of June 30, 2026
  * Insurance Application Submission**:** From June 6, 2026, to the end of June 16, 2026.
  * Insurance Policy Issuance**:** From June 6, 2026, to the end of June 30, 2026.


### **2. Applicable Products:**
  * All products except for **PRU – AN TAM BAO TIN**.


### **3. Promotional Gifts:**
  * **Got It E-voucher worth VND 1,500,000** for the first regular premium of an eligible policy with an annualized premium (APE) from VND 50 million and above.
  * **Got It E-voucher worth VND 500,000** for the first regular premium of an eligible policy with an annualized premium (APE) from VND 20 million to less than VND 50 million.


For more details about the promotion program, please click [here](https://www.msb.com.vn/msb-website/2026/06/BIBP_BC_0099.MSB-Q22026-June-Flash-Promotion-06.06.2026-16.06.2026.pdf).
Please contact our hotline at **1900 6083** or visit your nearest **MSB Branch/Transaction Office** for free consultation and support.

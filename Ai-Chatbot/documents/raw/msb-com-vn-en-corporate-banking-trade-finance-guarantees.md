Can credit limits based on direct cash flow commitments be granted to customers in all industries?
13/03/2026
No. Direct designated cash flow limits are not granted for customers operating in the construction sector.
Can financing be provided based on offtake contracts without a fixed contract value?
13/03/2026
Yes. It may be applied to export plans where the quantity of goods can be determined, such as in cashew processing or coffee wholesale/production industries.
Does MSB provide short-term foreign currency loans for import payments?
13/03/2026
Yes. However, customers must comply with the foreign exchange regulations of MSB and the State Bank of Vietnam (SBV).

## Frequently Asked Questions
Is it safe when using Apple Pay?
07/05/2026
Security and privacy are top priority of Apple. Your card information is not stored on the device or Apple’s servers. Instead, each account number is individually encrypted, and transaction details are securely stored in the system. All transactions require Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode. These features are designed specifically for secure payments and purchases using Apple Pay.
Why should you use MSB card for payments on Apple Pay?
07/05/2026
Convenience and easy: If you have multiple cards but don’t want to carry them all, MSB supports adding your MSB Mastercard/Visa to Apple Pay. Just use your mobile device or watch for safe and secure payments.
What kind of MSB cards can I use for Apple Pay?
07/05/2026
Mastercard or Visa credit/debit cards issued by MSB are active, not blocked, canceled, or terminated, and are still valid.

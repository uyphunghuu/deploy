# Terms and Conditions: CASA Growth Incentive Program for Affluent Retail Customers
08/06/2026
period of application 09/06/2026 - 31/08/2026  (Ongoing) 
promotional scope Applicable nationwide
Eligibility Affluent retail customers
**1. Promotion Program Name:** Promotion for Individual Customers in the Affluent Segment at MSB
**2. Program Period:** From June 09, 2026, to the end of August 31, 2026, or until a replacement notice is issued by MSB from time to time, whichever comes first, and MSB is not required to provide prior notice.
**3. Program Territory (Scope):** Nationwide
**4. Program Customers (Target audience eligible for program review):**
Customers who are individual customers belonging to the Affluent segment according to MSB’s regulations and simultaneously satisfy the following conditions:
  * Receive a notification from MSB about this promotion program via the Mobile App.
  * Are existing customers in the Affluent segment according to MSB’s regulations.
  * Have an Average Monthly Balance (AMB) in their VND payment account of less than 10,000,000 VND during the review month.
  * Are not included in the list of fraudulent, unusual, or rule-violating customers of MSB in any period.


**5. Promotional Goods and Services:** Customers who meet the program conditions will simultaneously enjoy the following benefits:  
| **Applicable Product**  | **Promotion**  |  
| --- | --- |  
| **Online VND Savings**  | An additional interest rate of +2.3%/year for 6-month online VND savings accounts opened during the program period. The bonus rate is applied to MSB’s listed interest rate at the time and may change according to MSB’s policies from time to time.  |  
| **Unsecured Loans**  | An interest rate reduction of 2%/year for 3 months on newly disbursed unsecured loans (excluding overdraft limits and credit cards) during the program period. The incentive level may change according to MSB’s policies from time to time.  |  
**6. Conditions to Receive Promotion (Target audience eligible to enjoy benefits):**
Customers will receive the promotion when they simultaneously meet all the following conditions:
  * Belong to the target audience defined in the regulations (under Section 4) of these Terms and Conditions.
  * Maintain an Average Monthly Balance (AMB) in their VND payment account of 11,000,000 VND or higher for 30 days during the reward evaluation period (under Section 7).
  * In case a customer holds multiple VND payment accounts at MSB, the VND payment account AMB will be calculated based on the total AMB of all the customer’s VND payment accounts at MSB.


**7. Timeline for Review Period, Reward Evaluation Period, and Implementation Method:**
**a) Timeline for customer review period and reward evaluation period:**  
| **Content**  |  **Customer Program Review Period** **(Customers eligible to join the program under Section 4)**  |  **Reward Evaluation Period** **(Customers eligible to receive benefits under Section 6)**  |  
| --- | --- | --- |  
| **Phase I**  | 01/05/2026 – 31/05/2026  | 09/06/2026 – 30/06/2026  |  
| **Phase II**  | 01/06/2026 – 30/06/2026  | 01/07/2026 – 31/07/2026  |  
| **Phase III**  | 01/07/2026 – 31/07/2026  | 01/08/2026 – 31/08/2026  |  
**b) Implementation Method:**
  * The assessment of reward eligibility is performed daily within each evaluation period. Customers will receive the promotion as soon as they fully meet the conditions specified under Section 6 or immediately upon receiving a congratulatory notification from MSB confirming their eligibility for the program benefits.
  * MSB compiles the list of eligible customers based on the database and information recorded on the system.
  * Validity period of the promotion: from the date the customer is determined eligible until the end of August 31, 2026.
  * Each customer can only apply the loan interest rate promotion to one (01) single loan during the program implementation.
  * Customers who satisfy the program conditions will enjoy the savings interest rate promotion for all new 6-month online savings accounts opened during the program period.
  * In case an invalid transaction, fraudulent transaction, or sign of program exploitation is detected, MSB reserves the right to refuse or revoke the granted promotion.


**8. Contact Point for Customer Inquiries Regarding the Promotion Program:**
Customers may contact MSB for assistance and inquiries related to the Program through the following channels:
  * Email: Mfirst247@msb.com.vn
  * Customer Care Hotline: 1800599999
  * MSB Branches/Transaction Offices nationwide

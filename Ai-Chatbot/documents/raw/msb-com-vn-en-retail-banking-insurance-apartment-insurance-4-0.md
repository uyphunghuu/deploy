Overview  Insurance Benefits  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide

 | Overview  | Apartment Insurance 4.0 is a comprehensive home insurance solution designed to protect your family’s most valuable asset against unexpected risks.  |  
| --- | --- |  
| Policy term  | 1 year  |  
| Premium payment term  | Annually  |  
| Coverage scope  | 
  * Fire, explosion, lightning
  * Storms, floods, burst water pipes
  * Theft, accidental damage, temporary accommodation expenses
  * Legal liability if incidents affect neighboring apartments

 |  
 |  
| Standard Plan  | (Based on Government regulations for mandatory fire and explosion insurance) 
  * 0.055% × apartment value (if the apartment has an automatic fire sprinkler system)
  * 0.1% × apartment value (if the apartment does not have an automatic fire sprinkler system)

 |  
| --- | --- |  
| Advanced Plan (Fixed liability)  | 
  * Physical damage to the apartment as regulated
  * Contents insurance: VND 400 million (maximum VND 20 million per item)
  * Third-party liability: VND 1.2 billion

 |  
| Premium Plan (Flexible liability)  | 
  * Physical damage to the apartment as regulated + Risk A
  * Contents insurance: VND 600 million (maximum VND 30 million per item)
  * Legal liability for neighboring apartments in case of fire caused by the homeowner

 |  
| Elite Plan (Flexible liability)  | 
  * Physical damage to the apartment as regulated + Risk A + Risk B
  * Contents insurance: VND 800 million (maximum VND 50 million per item)
  * Legal liability for neighboring apartments in case of fire caused by the homeowner

 |

## Frequently Asked Questions
[ Learn more ](https://www.msb.com.vn/en/contact-us-support/faqs/#retail-banking)
Am I required to purchase insurance for the property/apartment/vehicle during the loan term?
16/03/2026
Yes. Fire insurance for houses/apartments and insurance for transport vehicles not only secure the loan for the Bank but also protect the customer’s own interests.
Is insurance required for collateral during the loan period?
10/03/2026
Depending on the type of collateral. For example, machinery, equipment, or production lines may require insurance. Customers must maintain valid insurance coverage throughout the loan period.
Is vehicle insurance required during the loan period?
13/03/2026
Yes. Before disbursement, customers are required to purchase comprehensive vehicle insurance (physical damage insurance). The insurance must be maintained continuously throughout the loan period in accordance with the loan agreement.

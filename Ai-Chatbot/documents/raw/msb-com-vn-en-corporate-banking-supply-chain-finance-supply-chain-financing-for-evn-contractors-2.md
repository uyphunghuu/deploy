## Product information
Product Information  Eligibility & Conditions  Application Guide 
In an increasingly competitive market, participating in large-scale tenders such as those of Vietnam Electricity (EVN) requires businesses to maintain stable capital sources and flexible financial solutions. Understanding this need, MSB introduces a specialized financing solution package that enables businesses to access funding more easily, optimize cash flow, and strengthen financial capacity to successfully execute contracts.
**Flexible Financial Incentives – Tailored for Every Stage of Your Business**
**Before Obtaining an Output Contract** :
  * Businesses may still be granted a financing limit of up to 30% of total revenue from EVN over the past 12 months, with a maximum of VND 30 billion.
  * This financial solution helps businesses prepare resources and enhance their ability to win bids.


**After Obtaining an Output Contract**
  * Financing limits may reach up to 45% of the latest annual revenue, with a maximum of VND 100 billion.
  * Attractive financing ratios: 
    * Trading enterprises: up to 30% of revenue
    * Manufacturing and construction enterprises: up to 45% of revenue
    * Fast disbursement support, helping businesses ensure timely contract execution


Outstanding Benefits of the Program
  * Free guarantee issuance of up to VND 1.5 million, helping businesses optimize financial costs when participating in bidding.
  * Fast approval time – within 10 days, ensuring timely capital for project implementation.
  * Multiple funding purposes, including: 
    * Payment for raw materials and labor costs
    * Issuance of guarantees and Letters of Credit (L/C) for international payments
    * Flexible support for other corporate financial need
  * Special incentives when transacting with MSB 
    * 100% waiver of online transfer fees
    * Preferential foreign exchange rates for online FX transactions.


  * Applicable to both large enterprises and SMEs.
  * Financing value depends on the company’s most recent annual revenue and total revenue with EVN over the past 12 months.
  * Processing time is calculated from the moment complete documentation is received from the customer.


For consultation, please contact Hotline: +84 24 7307 5566 – Ext. 6157 (Supply Chain & Partnership Development Department) or leave your contact information.

What working capital loan products does the bank offer for EVN contractors?
11/03/2026
  * Loans under the EVN Contractor Supply Chain Financing product.
  * Working capital loans with a revolving credit limit of up to 12 months.


Does the bank provide unsecured credit for EVN contractors?
11/03/2026
Yes. The EVN Contractor Supply Chain Financing product is offered under an unsecured credit limit.
Besides unsecured financing, can I obtain financing secured by collateral?
12/02/2026
Yes. Businesses may obtain loans secured by collateral, with financing value of up to 280% of the appraised collateral value

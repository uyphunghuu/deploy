How can I adjust the transaction description?
15/03/2026
**You can submit a trace request to modify the description of a successful transfer from MSB by following these steps:**
  * **Step 1:** Log in to Internet Banking → Select **Accounts** → Choose the transaction account.
  * **Step 2:** Select **Transaction History** and locate the transfer that needs adjustment.
  * **Step 3:** Click **Trace** → Enter your modification request in the “Trace Content” field.


**Processing Time:** Maximum of 04 working days. Feedback will be sent via your registered phone number.
_**Note:** The receiving bank will be notified of the adjustment to inform the recipient._
I want to check my business account number?
19/03/2026
You can find out your account number through one of the following methods:
  * Method 1: Log in to the M-emobile app, select Account → Account inquiry, then select payment account to display your account number.  

  * Method 2: Access https://ebank.msb.com.vn, select account to check your account number.  

  * Method 3: You can visit the nearest MSB branch for assistance.


What should I do if the recipient has not received the funds?
15/03/2026
**If the recipient has not yet received the money, you can submit a trace request by following these steps:**
  * **Step 1:** Log in to Internet Banking → Select **Accounts** → Choose the transaction account.
  * **Step 2:** Select **Transaction History** and locate the specific transaction.
  * **Step 3:** Click **Trace** → Enter your request details in the “Content” field.


**Note:** Trace requests are processed within a maximum of 04 working days. You will receive the result via your registered phone number.

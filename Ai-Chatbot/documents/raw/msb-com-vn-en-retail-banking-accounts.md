Where can you use Apple Pay?
07/05/2026
You can use Apple Pay with your existing MSB Mastercard/Visa at any location that accepts payments via iPhone or Apple Watch. You can also use Apple Pay within your favorite apps or websites when you see the Apple Pay icon and contactless payment symbol.
How can I close my account at MSB?
18/03/2026
Customers can visit any MSB branch or transaction office nationwide to request account closure.
Why does the system say “Service is supported from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Please come back later” when I register for an account online?
12/03/2026
You are currently registering for an account outside of our supported service hours. To successfully open an account, please complete your registration between **07:00 AM and 10:00 PM** daily.
